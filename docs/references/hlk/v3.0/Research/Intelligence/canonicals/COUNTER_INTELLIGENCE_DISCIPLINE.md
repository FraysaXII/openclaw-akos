## 1. Purpose

Counter-intelligence in Holistika is the **PROTECT** guard over the CORPINT lifecycle: protecting
sources, methods, crown-jewel knowledge, and collection legality while intelligence is acquired,
processed, stored, recalled, and shared. It is not a separate "spy vs spy" program — it is the
discipline that keeps Research's collection and dissemination **legal, ethical, and survivable**.

absorb Ethics or Compliance doctrine.

## 3. Lifecycle hooks (where PROTECT fires)

| Stage | PROTECT question |
|:---|:---|
| ACQUIRE | Is collection legal and proportionate? Are sources protected? |
| PROCESS | Are analytic products free of source-identifying detail beyond need-to-know? |
| STORE | Are crown jewels at the correct access level? Mirrors deny anon/auth by default? |
| RECALL | Does retrieval respect AL + confidence gates? |
| SHARE | Is redaction complete before external register translation? |
