# PESTEL Analysis — per-letter viewpoint discipline

> **PROCESS-stage pillar.** Registered in `process_list.csv` as `hol_resea_dtp_315`. Applies at
> **per-prong synthesis**, not at source-ledger ingest.

## 1. Operator discipline (binding)

Analyze the **same prong subject** from **each letter's separate viewpoint** — six passes, not
one blended paragraph.

| Letter | Viewpoint question |
|:---|:---|
| **P** Political | What policy, regulatory, or geopolitical pressure touches this prong? |
| **E** Economic | What cost, ROI, or market-economics pressure touches this prong? |
| **S** Social | What workforce, adoption, or cultural friction touches this prong? |
| **T** Technological | What technology shift binds or threatens this prong? |
| **E** Environmental | What sustainability or resource footprint matters (or explicit N/A)? |
| **L** Legal | What legal or compliance bar constrains this prong? |

## 2. Output shape

In each `prong-p*.md` synthesis file, include a **PESTEL** section with **one bullet per letter**
(max 2 sentences each). Cite `SRC-*` IDs from the ledger where possible.
