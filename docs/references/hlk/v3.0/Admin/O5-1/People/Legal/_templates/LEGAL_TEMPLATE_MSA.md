# LEGAL_TEMPLATE_MSA — Master Services Agreement

> **Template — not legal advice.** This is a starting-draft Master Services Agreement (MSA) template aligned with Holistika's Branded House architecture. Counsel + operator must review every clause before signing. Bracketed `[VARIABLE]` placeholders are filled per-engagement.

> **Architectural anchor**: The contracting party is **always Holistika Research SL** (the sole legal entity). Sub-marks (Holistika R&S, Think Big, HLK Tech Lab) and product brands (MADEIRA, KiRBe, ENVOY) are **named in scope as services rendered**, never as contracting parties (per `BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE_2026-04.md` §1).

---

## MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "**Agreement**") is entered into as of `[EFFECTIVE_DATE]` (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**Holistika Research SL**, a Spanish *Sociedad Limitada* with registered office at `[REGISTERED_ADDRESS]`, NIF `[NIF]`, registered with the Registro Mercantil de Madrid under `[REGISTRATION_DETAILS]` ("**Holistika**" or the "**Service Provider**");

and

`[CLIENT_LEGAL_NAME]`, a `[CLIENT_ENTITY_TYPE]` with registered office at `[CLIENT_ADDRESS]`, `[CLIENT_TAX_ID_TYPE]` `[CLIENT_TAX_ID]` (the "**Client**").

Holistika and the Client are each a "**Party**" and collectively the "**Parties**".

## 1. Definitions

1.1. "**Services**" means the consulting, research, technology, training, and capability-building services to be rendered by Holistika under one or more Statements of Work executed under this Agreement, organised across Holistika's three operational delivery modes: (a) **Holistika R&S** (research-and-strategy services); (b) **Think Big** (strategy-operationalisation services); and (c) **HLK Tech Lab** (engineering and technical-integration services). Specific Services per engagement are defined in the applicable Statement of Work.

1.2. "**Statement of Work**" or "**SOW**" means a written statement of work executed by both Parties referencing this Agreement and setting out: scope, deliverables, timeline, fees, success criteria, applicable delivery mode, and any engagement-specific terms.

1.3. "**Deliverables**" means the work products described in an SOW.

1.4. "**Confidential Information**" has the meaning given in Section 7 below.

1.5. "**Intellectual Property Rights**" means all rights in patents, trademarks, designs, copyrights, database rights, trade secrets, know-how, and any similar rights, whether registered or unregistered, anywhere in the world.

## 2. Engagement of Services

2.1. From time to time the Parties may execute SOWs governed by this Agreement. Each SOW is a separate contract for the Services described therein, governed by this Agreement.

2.2. In the event of any conflict between this Agreement and an SOW, this Agreement controls **except** where the SOW expressly states it modifies a specific provision of this Agreement.

2.3. The applicable delivery mode (Holistika R&S / Think Big / HLK Tech Lab) is identified in each SOW. The contracting party for all engagements is Holistika Research SL regardless of delivery mode.

## 3. Fees and Payment

3.1. Fees for Services are specified in each SOW. Fee structures may be: (a) **scoped** (fixed-price for a defined deliverable); (b) **embedded** (recurring fee for continuous-presence engagement); or (c) **transformational** (multi-phase program fee, payable per phase).

3.2. Unless otherwise stated in an SOW, fees are exclusive of VAT and other applicable taxes, which are payable in addition.

3.3. Holistika invoices monthly in arrears unless an SOW specifies otherwise. Invoices are payable within `[PAYMENT_TERMS_DAYS]` days of invoice date.

3.4. Late payments accrue interest at the statutory rate per Spanish Law 3/2004 (or applicable equivalent for non-Spanish counterparties) from the due date until paid.

3.5. Out-of-pocket expenses (travel, materials, third-party software/data, etc.) are reimbursable when pre-approved by the Client and documented per Holistika's standard expense policy.

## 4. Term and Termination

4.1. This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated under this Section 4.

4.2. Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon `[TERMINATION_NOTICE_DAYS]` days' written notice; provided that any active SOW continues until completion or termination of that SOW.

4.3. Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately for material breach by the other Party that is not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice.

4.4. Sections that by their nature survive termination (including 7 Confidentiality, 8 IP, 9 Liability, 10 Indemnification, 11 General Provisions) survive termination.

## 5. Performance

5.1. Holistika performs Services with reasonable care, skill, and diligence consistent with prevailing industry standards for professional research, consulting, and technology-engineering services.

5.2. The Client cooperates reasonably with Holistika's performance, including providing timely access to Client personnel, information, and systems where required by an SOW.

5.3. Each SOW specifies acceptance criteria for Deliverables. Deliverables are deemed accepted when (a) the Client provides written acceptance; or (b) the Client uses the Deliverable in a productive setting; or (c) `[ACCEPTANCE_DEEMED_DAYS]` days pass after delivery without written rejection by the Client.

## 6. Personnel

6.1. Holistika determines which personnel perform Services. Holistika may engage subcontractors (with notice to Client where the SOW specifies) but remains responsible for their performance.

6.2. The Parties acknowledge that Holistika's personnel may also be engaged with other clients; provided that Holistika maintains appropriate confidentiality and conflict-of-interest controls.

## 7. Confidentiality

7.1. "**Confidential Information**" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "**Disclosing Party**") to the other Party (the "**Receiving Party**") in connection with this Agreement that is marked confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given its nature and circumstances of disclosure.

7.2. The Receiving Party (a) holds Confidential Information in confidence; (b) uses it solely for purposes of performance under this Agreement; (c) discloses it only to its personnel and advisors who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective; and (d) protects it with at least the same care it uses to protect its own confidential information of like importance, but in no case less than reasonable care.

7.3. Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes generally available to the public through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was rightfully in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure; (c) is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality obligation; or (d) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information.

7.4. Confidentiality obligations under this Section 7 survive for `[CONFIDENTIALITY_SURVIVAL_YEARS]` years after termination, except for trade secrets which survive for so long as they remain trade secrets.

## 8. Intellectual Property

8.1. **Holistika Background IP.** Holistika owns and retains all Intellectual Property Rights in (a) its methodologies, frameworks, tools, software (including MADEIRA, KiRBe, ENVOY, and any other Holistika products), templates, know-how, and pre-existing materials; and (b) any improvements, enhancements, or derivative works thereof. Holistika grants the Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free licence to use Holistika Background IP to the extent embedded in Deliverables and necessary for the Client's internal use of those Deliverables.

8.2. **Client Background IP.** The Client owns and retains all Intellectual Property Rights in its pre-existing materials provided to Holistika in connection with the Services. The Client grants Holistika a non-exclusive licence to use Client Background IP solely for performing the Services.

8.3. **Foreground IP.** Subject to Section 8.1 (Holistika Background IP retention), Intellectual Property Rights in Deliverables created specifically for the Client under an SOW vest in the Client upon full payment of all fees due for that SOW. Where an SOW expressly states otherwise (e.g., where Holistika develops a methodology refinement that becomes part of Holistika's product stack), the SOW controls.

8.4. **Brand IP.** Holistika retains exclusive ownership of `Holistika`, `Holistika R&S`, `Think Big`, `HLK Tech Lab`, `MADEIRA`, `MADEIRA Agent`, `KiRBe`, `ENVOY`, and all related trademarks, design marks, and trade dress. Nothing in this Agreement grants the Client any right to use Holistika's trademarks except as expressly authorised by Holistika in writing or for limited factual reference (e.g., "performed by Holistika") in the Client's internal records.

## 9. Limitation of Liability

9.1. **Cap.** Each Party's aggregate liability under this Agreement (whether in contract, tort, statute, or otherwise) is limited to the total fees paid or payable by the Client under the SOW(s) giving rise to the claim during the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9.2. **Excluded damages.** Neither Party is liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, exemplary, or punitive damages, or for loss of profits, revenue, business, anticipated savings, data, goodwill, or reputation, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

9.3. **Carve-outs.** The limitations in 9.1 and 9.2 do not apply to: (a) breach of Section 7 (Confidentiality); (b) breach of Section 8 (Intellectual Property); (c) the Client's payment obligations; (d) a Party's gross negligence, fraud, or wilful misconduct; or (e) liability that cannot be excluded under applicable mandatory law.

## 10. Indemnification

10.1. **Holistika indemnification.** Holistika defends, indemnifies, and holds the Client harmless from third-party claims that the Deliverables, as delivered by Holistika, infringe a third party's Intellectual Property Rights. This indemnification does not apply to claims arising from: (a) Client modifications to Deliverables; (b) combination of Deliverables with materials not provided by Holistika; (c) Client Background IP; or (d) use of Deliverables outside the scope contemplated by the applicable SOW.

10.2. **Client indemnification.** The Client defends, indemnifies, and holds Holistika harmless from third-party claims arising from Client Background IP or the Client's use of Deliverables outside their intended scope.

## 11. General Provisions

11.1. **Governing law and forum.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of Spain. The courts of Madrid have exclusive jurisdiction over disputes, except that either Party may seek injunctive relief in any court of competent jurisdiction.

11.2. **Notices.** Notices must be in writing and delivered by registered mail or signed electronic delivery (e.g., DocuSign) to the addresses on the cover page or such other addresses as a Party may designate in writing.

11.3. **Independent contractors.** The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing creates a partnership, joint venture, employment, or agency relationship.

11.4. **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent, except that either Party may assign to a successor in interest in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, with notice to the other Party.

11.5. **Entire agreement.** This Agreement (together with any executed SOWs and the Data Processing Agreement, if applicable) constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous understandings, oral or written.

11.6. **Amendment.** This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

11.7. **Severability.** If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force, and the invalid provision is reformed to the minimum extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable.

11.8. **Force majeure.** Neither Party is liable for failure or delay in performance to the extent caused by events beyond reasonable control (war, terrorism, natural disaster, government action, pandemic, internet/utility outage). The affected Party gives prompt notice and resumes performance as soon as reasonably possible.

11.9. **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature, each of which is deemed an original and all of which together constitute one agreement.
