# BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE — Branded House posture (rewritten I66 P1)

> **Status — Active (rewritten Initiative 66 P1; 2026-05-08).** Supersedes the 2026-04-08 v1.0 draft, which left the question "is Holistica or Holistika first entity, or is the umbrella name a separate filing target?" open. **Decision [D-IH-66-A](../../../../wip/planning/66-brand-vision-ops-sweep/decision-log.md#d-ih-66-a) closes that question: Branded House.** This document encodes the resolved architecture and the resulting trademark filing scope.

## 1. Resolved architecture — Branded House

Holistika is a **Branded House** (McKinsey-style — single legal entity, single umbrella brand, multiple operational sub-marks, multiple product brands), not a House of Brands (Procter & Gamble-style — separate legal entities per brand). This decision was driven by:

- **Cost.** A single Sociedad Limitada (SL) is materially cheaper to operate than three SLs (each requiring its own books, audit, legal counsel, payroll, and corporate filings).
- **Operator advice.** Tax + legal + ENISA advisers consistently advised one entity for the founder-incorporation phase. Three entities are an option for the future scaling phase (4-5 years out) when revenue concentration in any single arm justifies the operational overhead.
- **Brand strategy.** The umbrella "Holistika" carries the trust + research-grade signal; sub-marks ("Think Big", "HLK Tech Lab") carry the per-context specialization. Splitting umbrella across legal entities dilutes umbrella trust without commensurate brand benefit.
- **Trademark efficiency.** A single ownership entity ("Holistika Research SL") owns all marks. Cross-licensing complexity vanishes.

| Layer | Form | Trademark scope |
|:---:|:---|:---|
| **1 — Legal entity** | Holistika Research SL (sole entity; Madrid; Sociedad Limitada) | Corporate registration with the Registro Mercantil de Madrid (already filed); not a trademark filing — this is a corporate-form registration. |

## 2. Frozen filing strings

### 2.1 Layer 2 — umbrella

**Filing string (Latin-1 prose, for legal forms):** `Holistika`  
**Filing string (stylized canonical wordmark, for design mark):** `HOLÍSTIKA Research` (with diacritic on Í; per [`BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md`](../../Marketing/Brand/BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md) §2.2)

The diacritic-Í wordmark is filed as a **separate design mark** alongside the plain word mark. This protects both: (a) the plain "Holistika" word in trade, (b) the visual canonical with diacritic. Filing both is a standard EUIPO + OEPM practice for brands with Latin-extended wordmarks.

**Why one umbrella and not "Holistica" + "Holistika" both:** D-IH-66-A resolves any prior diacritic ambiguity. The plain word is "Holistika" (with K — not "Holistica" with C, which would have read as a Spanish-only spelling). The K is canonical; the diacritic-Í on the wordmark is a stylistic + visual decision, not a separate brand.

### 2.2 Layer 3 — sub-marks

**Filing string (Holistika R&S):** `Holistika R&S` (paired wordmark; "R&S" never stands alone). Filed as a separate sub-mark to enable separate Nice-class targeting if Holistika R&S evolves into a distinct revenue line.

**Filing string (Think Big):** `Think Big` (plain wordmark; collision-prone phrase, filed national-only in Spain via OEPM). Risk: "Think Big" is a generic phrase common in motivational + business literature. **Mitigation: file in Nice 35 + 42 only (consulting + research services); accept narrow scope; expect some objections during opposition window.** Brand-only protection in EU via use-in-trade.

**Filing string (HLK Tech Lab):** `HLK Tech Lab` (paired wordmark; "HLK" never stands alone in the brand sub-mark — per [`BRAND_ABBREVIATIONS.md`](../../Marketing/Brand/BRAND_ABBREVIATIONS.md) §2.1). EUIPO + OEPM filing.

### 2.3 Layer 4 — product brands

**MADEIRA + MADEIRA Agent:**

- Filing string: `MADEIRA` (plain wordmark) + `MADEIRA Agent` (paired wordmark for the Agent variant).
- **High collision risk:** Madeira is a Portuguese island + a wine region + a wood type. The mark is unprotectable in Nice 33 (wines), 32 (beers), 30 (food), 39 (transport), 41 (entertainment), 43 (hospitality), 44 (medical). **Mitigation: file in Nice 9 (computer software) + 42 (software-as-a-service) only.** This is the narrowest possible scope but matches actual product use (technology platform, not consumer goods).
- Both EUIPO + OEPM filings to anchor protection in EU (large market) + Spain (home market).

**KiRBe:**

- Filing string: `KiRBe` (mixed-case wordmark; the "iRBe" component evokes "internal RAG-based engine" but we don't claim that meaning publicly).
- Nice 9 + 42.
- EUIPO + OEPM.

**ENVOY:**

- Filing string: `ENVOY` (plain uppercase wordmark).
- Nice 9 + 42.
- **Collision risk:** ENVOY is used by other technology companies (e.g., Envoy Proxy, Envoy real-estate). **Mitigation: opposition window is the operator-engagement period; counsel monitors for live conflicts.** EUIPO filing first; OEPM second.

**InfraMonitor + Financial Analyst (coming soon):**

- Not filed in P4. Filing scope reserved for a later initiative when commercial validation justifies the filing fees.
- Reserved domain names (`*.holistikaresearch.com` subdomains) hold defensive position.

## 4. Filing order (sequenced by I66 P4)

1. **Clearance searches** (EUIPO + OEPM databases × 7 marks; documented per mark).
2. **Filing strategy decisions** per mark (EUIPO vs OEPM vs both; word-only vs word-and-design).
3. **Filing prep packets** (mark + classes + applicant data + power of attorney boilerplate).
4. **Ready-to-sign EUIPO TM-1 forms + OEPM equivalent forms** (5 EUIPO + 2 OEPM = 7 forms; per [`TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md`](TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md) §5).
5. **Operator handoff** to filing agent (the actual submission with EUIPO/OEPM is operator-driven; not within I66 scope).
6. **Post-filing** (oppositions + objections + registration) — tracked separately as `process_list.csv` row "TRADEMARK Filing Lifecycle Management" (created I66 P3) under Legal Counsel + Brand Manager.

## 5. Per-jurisdiction fees (provisional, requires filing-day validation per Official-Fee Rule)

**EUIPO** (per the EUIPO 2026 fee schedule; subject to filing-day validation):

- Word mark, single class: ~€850
- Word mark, additional classes: +€50 per class up to 3, then +€150 per class beyond 3
- Design mark, single class: same as word
- Word + design as separate filings: roughly 2× the single-mark fee

**OEPM (Spain national)** (per the OEPM 2026 fee schedule; subject to filing-day validation):

- Word mark, single class: ~€144 (significantly cheaper than EUIPO)
- Additional classes: +€90 per class

**Provisional total** (subject to per-mark scope finalization in P4):

- ~€5,000–€7,000 EUIPO total (5 marks × 1–2 classes)
- ~€500–€800 OEPM total (2 standalone marks × 1–2 classes; plus duplicates of Holistika + MADEIRA in OEPM at ~€144 each)
- **Adviser/service fees separate** per Official-Fee Rule (not bundled).

## 6. Official-Fee Rule (preserved from 2026-04-08 v1.0)

No trademark budget assumption is treated as valid until the official-office fee source has been captured for the target jurisdiction. The current working rule:

- **Official office fees** (EUIPO + OEPM) and **adviser service fees** (lawyer + filing agent) are always logged separately.
- Any quoted figure from a provider that cannot be tied to the filing office, jurisdiction, and class count remains provisional.
- The filing agent (lawyer + IP firm) issues a written quote that breaks out (a) office fees, (b) preparation fees, (c) submission fees, (d) post-filing monitoring fees, (e) opposition-response retainer.

## 7. What is protected separately (preserved from 2026-04-08 v1.0; expanded)

These remain separate filing decisions, even within a Branded House:

- **Legal entity name** (Holistika Research SL) — corporate registration; not a trademark filing.
- **Umbrella brand wordmark** (Holistika; HOLÍSTIKA design) — primary trademark target.
- **Sub-mark wordmarks** (Holistika R&S, Think Big, HLK Tech Lab) — separate filings; separate Nice scopes.
- **Product brand wordmarks** (MADEIRA, MADEIRA Agent, KiRBe, ENVOY) — separate filings; narrowest Nice scope per mark (collision-driven).
- **Visual logo system** (Hi monogram + HOLÍSTIKA wordmark — per [`BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md`](../../Marketing/Brand/BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md)) — design marks that supplement (not substitute) word marks.
- **Methodology / publication titles** (e.g., a future paper or framework name) — filed separately if/when commercial validation justifies; not in I66 scope.
