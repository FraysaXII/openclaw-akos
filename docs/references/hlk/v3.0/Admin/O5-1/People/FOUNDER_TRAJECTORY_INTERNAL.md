# Founder Trajectory — Internal companion to FOUNDER_BIO.md

> **Internal-register companion** to the public `FOUNDER_BIO.md`. This file holds the founder's full personal trajectory with real public-employer names, the intellectual-influence narrative, and the method-anchor lineage. It is for **operator + agent eyes only**: never reproduced verbatim on customer-facing surfaces, never shared via decks, never quoted in proposals. Public bio variants live in `FOUNDER_BIO.md` (anonymized per `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`).
>
> Reason for this file vs editing `FOUNDER_BIO.md` directly: the canonical bio at that path is `register: external`, `status: active`, governed by `SOP-PEOPLE_FOUNDER_BIO_001`, and serves decks and press materials with a strict anonymization posture. Plan D-12-9 calls for an **internal-only** trajectory store with brand-jargon allowed; that intent is fulfilled here without clobbering an active external canonical.

## 1. Why this file exists (governance frame)

Three classes of evidence about the founder coexist in the AKOS system:

| Class | File | Posture |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Public-safe bio variants (long / medium / short / one-line / per-audience) | [FOUNDER_BIO.md](FOUNDER_BIO.md) | external, anonymized employer history |
| Filed legal facts (entity formation, mercantil registry, CNAE, IAE, banking) | [Legal/FOUNDER_FACT_PATTERN_RELATED_ENTITIES.md](Legal/FOUNDER_FACT_PATTERN_RELATED_ENTITIES.md) | internal, GOI/POI ref-id only for private entities |
| Personal trajectory + intellectual-influence narrative (this file) | this file | internal, public-employer names allowed; private-third-party names redacted per SOP-HLK_TRANSCRIPT_REDACTION_001 |

The public bio remains the source of truth for any external surface; this file is consulted *only* by the agent and the operator when shaping internal artefacts that need the real method-lineage (e.g. when explaining to internal collaborators why a specific discipline is anchored where).

**Forbidden uses of this file:**

- Quoting any line on a customer-pack surface (proposal, deck, tarification, CDC feasibility shape, discovery questionnaire).
- Sending its content over external channels (email to client, slide narration notes shared with counterparties, recruiter pitches).
- Reproducing employer names on any surface tagged `register: external`.

**Permitted uses:**

- Informing the founder's voice in customer-pack prose (the *intuition* survives; the *attribution* does not).
- Refreshing the public bio in `FOUNDER_BIO.md` when employment history changes (operator-driven, governed by `SOP-PEOPLE_FOUNDER_BIO_001`).
- Internal team onboarding and advisor briefings under NDA, when the public bio's anonymization layer is insufficient.
- Sourcing the slide-04 method anchors (D-12-3) by name, where the customer-pack surface only renders the method names without the employer attribution.

## 2. Personal trajectory (real names; public-employer only)

### Multiassistance — Business Analyst

December 2008 - June 2011. Lead, monitor, and coordinate the work of the French Insurance B2B Invoicing team, working with Pacifica and GCE. Co-creating Business Data Catalog, Data flow, and leading the analysis of functional requirements; reporting KPIs; building tables in Excel and SQL. Reporting to the France delegation's COO and CFO.

This is the period when *making operations legible* became the founder's guiding instinct. The team-management discipline (lead-monitor-coordinate as a tripartite verb-set) anchors the *"trois équipes, un seul objectif"* framing later used at Holistika.

### IBM — Business Analyst

July 2012 - July 2015. Data Modeling and Data Design Scheme for Global Sales Funnel performances, reporting to IBM Global Operations, Program Management, and Data Management teams. Daily progress on transactional databases via IBM Cognos, CIB processes, Excel/SQL and SharePoint, in coordination with every project team.

Created a project in SQL and VBA that allowed Managers to calculate FTEs for their teams based on real-time performance and contract signings. Sole responsible for Data Modeling on this project.

HR-side training engagement: provided Excel training to IBM Spain and its European delegation on a quarterly basis, covering Analysis of Functional Requirements, Data Catalog, Data flow, Data Quality Control Models.

This is where the **data-quality discipline** intuition was formed: the realisation that data-modelling decisions made before any analytics work shapes whether downstream insight is possible at all. Slide-04 method anchor #1 (data-quality discipline) traces here.

### BICG — Strategy Consultant / Change Manager

June 2015 - July 2016. Member of the design and conception committee for a national top-3 legal branch's strategic plan to be used from 2016 to 2019, including brand management, market research, business plan, HR and operations. Design of the internal process structure of the Generali Tower and the Generali Academy located in Italy. Leading IT Strategic Plan for Generali Academy and reporting performance to the brand's CTO. Support for international expansion and ESG strategies for several clients.

This is the **multinational-governance** intuition: the realisation that a coherent operating model travels across legal entities only when its primitives (process, role, decision-authority) are codified before they are deployed. Slide-04 method anchor #2 (multinational-governance discipline) traces here.

### Freelance — Strategy Advisor (concurrent)

June 2016 - March 2023. Concurrent freelance work alongside the employed positions below.

- Managing Partner: creation of company B with the brand and its expansion plan — digital strategy, corporate strategy with organization chart, departments, processes, e-commerce, and a franchise of stores with several products and services simultaneously across three stores in Madrid and Valencia.
- Strategy Consultant: international projects across various industries with EMEA clients.
- Data Consultant: projects covering Data Analytics, Data Governance, Master Data Management, IT Asset Management, and Development.

The franchise-scaling experience cited in `FOUNDER_BIO.md` §1 ("a small initial capital base into a multi-location franchise model with seven venues") is anchored here; counterparty names redacted per the public bio's anonymization rule.

### Econocom — IT Project Manager

February 2017 - February 2018. Leading IT teams and their projects to deliver services to EMEA clients, by implementing the ITIL v3 framework while directly reporting to the COO and CTO. Analyse and report KPIs and SLAs directly to the client; execute root-cause analysis; report status to client and internal staff. Services included: creation of user systems and company systems through Active Directory, ServiceNow CMDB, Citrix, ServiceNow ITSM. Recurrent processes: Analysis of Functional Requirements, Data Catalog, Data flow, and Data Quality Control Models to design telecommunication channels for help-desk service to 3 clients with international coverage in 6 languages each (French, English, Spanish, Italian, German, Dutch).

This is where **PMP-to-software discipline** crystallised: the operating realisation that classical project-management primitives (RACI, SLA, root-cause, KPI cadence) translate near-losslessly to software-delivery contexts when the primitives are themselves codified as artefacts rather than as meeting practice. Slide-04 method anchor #3 (PMP-to-software discipline) traces here.

### Volvo — Finance Operations Data Analyst

March 2018 - March 2020. Optimization of Order-to-Cash accounting operations, including creation of an FTE calculator available to Global Managers, harmonization of O2C accounting processes, and mapping of processes for clarity of the COO and CFO of Volvo Global and Spain. Co-creation of KPIs for all entities globally for a harmonized Data Governance and Data Management framework. Monitoring the Banking & ESG activity of Spain, Belgium, France, Portugal, USA, and Sweden. Global SPOC Order-to-Cash (O2C) for CIB/SCIB processes.

The *"camion bloqué"* anecdote (a key training story in the founder's voice corpus, cited in `voice-corpus.md` §1.4) traces here: the realisation that *single-point-of-knowledge* is the operating risk that survives every reorganisation, and that the only durable counter-measure is process-level legibility.

### GMA Corporate — Data Analytics Project Manager

October 2021 - February 2022. Freelance E2E project to analyse the work and processes of an investment fund's Asset Management team, creating the company's official financial report. All finance processes were reworked, including Banking & Investment-related processes (SCIB/CIB, factoring, financial data flow, Data Quality Models for traceability). Tools: Data Design Scheme, Data Modeling, Information Security Framework, IT Risk Management Framework, DMBOK, Agile PMP (PMBOK), Pen Testing, Data Catalog, Data flow, Data Quality Control Models, Master Data Management, Power BI, PowerApp, Dataverse, Dynamics, Navision, Microsoft Power Automate, SharePoint/Teams.

This is the **financial-traceability** intuition that informs the cdc-feasibility-shape framework's litige module today: every line of currency that crosses a system boundary needs an unbroken traceability chain back to the originating decision, or the cost shows up as a litige later.

### Europ Assistance / Generali — Data Governance Project Manager

April 2022 - August 2023. Tasked to lead the Data Governance Project between Europ Assistance Spain and the Generali Group, starting with promoting Insurance processes. Created the company's architecture and selected its components, launching projects including Data Management, Benchmark, RFP, and management of external specialized vendors. Implemented a Data Governance project replacing a previous external vendor, appointing Chief Data Users, Data Owners, Data Stewards, and Data System Owners. Led the integration of architecture data with the group architecture by launching a parallel project with the Group CDO and his project team. Led a project to create a hybrid architecture by moving key components into Azure. Implemented an Information Security Framework (ISO 27001, GDPR, NIST, SWIFT, LOPD).

This is the **regulator-aware operating-model** intuition. The compliance posture (article 20 RGPD; data-residency answers; access-governance) that the SUEZ proposal addresses head-on traces directly to the Generali Group integration discipline.

### RCD Legal — Data Governance Project Manager

November 2023 - current. Lead the efforts to create and run a new Data Governance Department and a PMO. Strategy: planned and executed an end-to-end Data Management plan including architecture, modeling, warehousing, transformation, quality, security, roles, and reporting. Utilised data profiling techniques to support the remediation of data quality issues. Managed the SQL server for data warehousing. Led a project to create a hybrid architecture by moving key components into Azure. PMO & ITIL: created a PMO to standardise and unify the project-based model of the company; implemented ITIL v4 for IT Department alignment, including migrating the CMDB provider to in-house processes. Designed and implemented a reporting framework extracting data from Zoho's PostgreSQL and the company's SQL Server, transforming in Dataverse and visualising in Power BI. Personalised reports for three stakeholder levels (managers, directors, partners). Standardised semantic models. Pyspark, Azure Data Lake, and Purview as the next-incremental layer.

The current full-time engagement. The cadence patterns the founder uses with operators (e.g. *"on découpe les tâches"*, the *"manuel d'utilisateur"* discipline, the FTE-calculator instinct) are continuously refreshed here.

The founder maintains professional acquaintances across the firms named in §2, several of whom are connected on LinkedIn. **None of these connections are commercially active or callable today.** They constitute *intellectual influence on method* — patterns of thought the founder absorbed and now uses — not a *callable network of senior practitioners*.

The plan's D-12-2 revision retires the *"réseau de praticiens senior mobilisable"* phrasing from customer slide 03 because:
