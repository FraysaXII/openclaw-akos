# FOUNDER_METHODOLOGY_VERSIONING — v0 to v3.0 lineage + 8 founder principles

> Authored I70 P9 (§9.5) per plan section 9. Codifies the v0-to-v3.0 founder methodology lineage + 8 founder principles distilled from the operator's curated I70-trigger prompt. Per **H3 ratification**: this canonical uses `operator` / `founder` framing (not personal name); the founder name `Fayçal Njoya` appears explicitly only in `FOUNDER_CORPUS_INVENTORY.md` (sibling) where CV-file inventories make anonymization performative.

## 1. The thesis

Holistika the company is the commercial vehicle. What the founder actually built (and continues to extend) is a **versioned methodology** that moved from v0 (unpaid-consulting era) through v1 (brand-as-authority-shield) + v2.x (breakthrough-driven re-versioning) to v3.0 (AI-coexistence operationalization). The methodology is non-replicable as a moat (per `KM_CHANNEL_VALUE_NARRATIVE.md` §2 Investor lens) — a competitor cannot backfill it.

This canonical timestamps the lineage + cites the 8 founder principles that operate the methodology. Every other I70 canonical references this one as **doctrinal substrate**.

## 3. The 8 founder principles (load-bearing across all I70 phases)

Eight principles distilled from the operator's curated I70-trigger prompt (per F-70 "every word in my prompt is curated"). Each is referenced by the I70 plan's §"Founder principles register" + becomes a section anchor here.

### 2.1 — AI-coexistence as design constraint

> *"Sarah Connor must have felt when she saw Arnold S. for the same time as Terminator 2 … ensuring Natural and Artificial Intelligence can coexist."*

Automation is built so the human keeps judgment; the application calculates, the human validates. Worked example: SUEZ Slide 08 four-step flow (Lecture → Composition → Revue → Soumission) where **Revue** is the human-validation gate.

Anchors: P5 (anti-AI-tone debias rationale per `BRAND_COPYWRITING_DISCIPLINE.md`); deferred I71 (CICD + AI-ops baseline) ethical-automation rule extension.

### 2.2 — Automation second-order accountability (CSOLT lesson)

> *"On 2014, as I worked on IBM Watson AI … sales process design … automate 80% of the European CSOLT … big lay-off. 2015! Imagine my face when I saw the same thing happen post 2023."*

The engineer is responsible for **who-loses-what** when the process they design ships. Codified in `ETHICAL_AUTOMATION_POSTURE.md` (sibling canonical at `People/Ethics/canonicals/`). Anchors: every automation-initiative decision gate (P5 Brand sub-discipline + P8 SMO + P10.5 ERP TSX scaffolds + I71 CICD ops).

### 2.3 — Anticipatory preparation

> *"v1 was when I understood it was a tone, and went all out to prepare for the future … not personal, but the world actually transforming … hone my skills to max."*

Holistika as multi-disciplinary preparation kit gathered before the disciplines mainstreamed. Justifies the **breadth** of the v3.0 vault (`Admin/O5-1/<area>/<role>/` per WORKSPACE_BLUEPRINT_HOLISTIKA §2). Anchors: every role-folder matters even when sparsely populated today.

### 2.4 — Brand-as-authority-shield

> *"Started versioning MY OWN understanding of things and putting an umbrella/brand in between for authority … first organigram of Bâtard has Holistika logo on it, but I had nothing solid still."*

The umbrella precedes the proof. Holistika the brand existed before Holistika the company; Bâtard's organigram already carried the mark. Justifies versioning **personal understanding** under a brand authority. Anchors: this canonical's v0-to-v3.0 lineage; `BRAND_VISION.md`; `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md` Branded House structure.

### 2.5 — Learn-by-doing-for-free

> *"People from N1 sources … kept asking me to give them insight on any random processes … across lots of sectors … I never said no, even if I knew it wouldn't get paid."*

Unpaid consulting as deliberate learning strategy. The v0 corpus (CV PDFs, sector breadth, process apprenticeship) is the materialized output of this principle. Justifies the **diversity** of the GOI/POI register classes (operator has worked across sectors, sizes, formal/informal engagements); explains why a generous initial inventory is proportionate.

### 2.6 — Breakthrough-driven re-versioning

> *"v2 … changing its name as we got new 'breakthroughts' as we call them."*

Every methodology jump triggers a new version; versions are not time-based, they are **insight-based**. Anchors `LOGIC_CHANGE_LOG.md` row schema (sibling canonical) + the v2.x → v3.0 jump. Each row in LOGIC_CHANGE_LOG records: breakthrough_id + date + insight + version_increment + canonicals_added_or_modified.

### 2.7 — Computational-capacity tipping point

> *"We didn't have computational capacity to record everything we said and did but believe I wouldn't be doing 40% of what I do now if it wasn't for that moment … We only had AI in 2.4 onwards."*

Pre-v2.4 was tacit-knowledge-loss era; post-v2.4 the missing computational capacity is what AI provides. **The agent (current state) IS the v3.0 computational capacity** reading the v0-to-v2.7 corpus. This principle anchors:
- Phase 4 §14 (agent-only-content channel pattern: R&L hosts v3.0 in Drive precisely so the agent has reading access without affecting upstream).
- Phase 4.8 MADEIRA-AKOS reserved folder (D-IH-70-V codification: AIC = category for non-MADEIRA AI agents; MADEIRA = L6 founder-companion productization). Per the operator's P2.5 audit Q2 expansion: today's Cursor-agent interactions are MADEIRA's empirical proof point.
- HLK_ERP_ARCHITECTURE §8 — AKOS-complete-enough trigger that gates MADEIRA productization.

### 2.8 — Process apprenticeship

> *"I worked on a lot of projects to get the hold I have now on lots of processes."*

Depth in ops doctrine comes from accumulated project exposure, not formal training. Explains why `process_list.csv` is the canonical operational record (every process has a `dtp_*` apprenticeship lineage) and why the SOP-META process management discipline is so strict (per `SOP-META_PROCESS_MGMT_001.md`).

- **v3.0** is the active version (post-I70-completion will be v3.1 per operator-stated trigger).
- **Future versions** are insight-driven per principle 2.6. When a breakthrough triggers a version jump, this canonical updates §2 with a new row + cross-link to `LOGIC_CHANGE_LOG.md`.
- **Naming** uses semantic version-style (major.minor): major = foundational change; minor = methodology refinement.
- **The operator (founder) is the sole authority** on version increments. AKOS canonicals codify decisions; only the founder mints version numbers.
