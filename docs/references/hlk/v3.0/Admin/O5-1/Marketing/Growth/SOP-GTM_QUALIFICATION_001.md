## Purpose

Run a **disciplined 90-day internal proof** of GTM motions for Holistika as a **services firm**, tied to **`holistika_gtm_dtp_001`** in [`process_list.csv`](../../../../../../compliance/process_list.csv).

## Scope

Covers **segment hypotheses**, **inbound/agency** motions, and **metrics**—not App Store product launch tasks (those remain under existing GTM children).

## Procedure

1. **Charter:** Confirm north-star metric and segment priority (see Initiative 14 [`phase-0-charter.md`](../../../../../../../wip/planning/14-holistika-internal-gtm-mops/reports/phase-0-charter.md)).
2. **Qualification criteria:** Document ICP fit, disqualifiers, and handoff to BD (see **SOP-GTM_BD_HANDOFF_001**).
3. **Weekly review:** Use **SOP-GTM_WEEKLY_METRICS_REVIEW_001** for cadence.
4. **Closeout:** Decision to extend, pivot segment, or promote playbooks to additional `process_list` rows (operator-approved tranche).

## Roles

- **CMO / Growth Manager:** Accountable for proof outcomes.
- **PMO:** Facilitates metrics forum.
