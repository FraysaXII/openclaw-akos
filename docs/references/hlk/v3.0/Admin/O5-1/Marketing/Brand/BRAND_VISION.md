## 1. Internal preamble (private; never shipped externally)

This section captures the founder's internal articulation in unredacted form. It is **not** rendered on `boilerplate/vision/`. Audience: founder + cleared collaborators + cursor agents that need full context for prose generation. Per [`akos-brand-baseline-reality.mdc`](../../../../../../../.cursor/rules/akos-brand-baseline-reality.mdc) (created I66 P2), prose generated from this preamble must translate to the external register before reaching public surfaces.

The internal motivation: **Holistika exists because most companies operate without seeing themselves clearly.** They have data they don't query, processes they don't measure, conversations they don't capture, decisions they don't trace. They run fast and break things until the breakage becomes the operating system.

The CORPINT background (founder's prior co-founder role; prior CIO/CDO/COO/Chief Strategist roles; the methodology that took a fitness venue from concept to 7 sites at +€250K each on €50K initial capital) taught one thing: structured intelligence collection + protected intel + audience-baselined communication = capital efficiency. Apply the same discipline to corporate operations and you get a 5-10× efficiency multiple over equivalent unstructured operations.

Holistika's job is to bring that discipline to companies that would never call themselves "intelligence operations" — SMEs, mid-market firms, EU-funded startups — by translating it into terms they read as professional research, structured methodology, evidence-based strategy.

The translation is not bait-and-switch; it's audience-baseline-reality. We are an intelligence operation in our practice; we are a research-and-strategy firm in our customer-facing register. Both are true; both are correct; the dual-register matrix ([`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`](BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md)) makes it operational.

## 2. Public vision (canonical for boilerplate `/vision/`)

<!-- public-vision:start -->

### Why Holistika exists

Most companies operate without seeing themselves clearly. They generate data they never query, run processes they never measure, hold conversations they never capture, and make decisions they cannot trace. The cost is not visible until it compounds — and by then it is the operating system.

Holistika exists to give companies a clearer view of their own operation, and the disciplines to keep that view honest as they grow.

### What we change

We change the inside of a company, not the outside. We do not invent your market. We do not redesign your product. We work on the layer between the work and the company that is doing it: how research informs strategy, how strategy guides execution, and how execution produces the next research question. When that layer is clear and disciplined, the company can scale; when it is foggy, every other investment leaks.

### How we change it

Three operational arms working as a flywheel.

**Holistika Research & Strategy** asks the questions and produces the frameworks. We are research-led, not opinion-led. Our frameworks are evidence-based, externally-tested, and revised when execution reveals the limits.

**Think Big** brings those frameworks into your operation as a consulting engagement. We are practitioners, not advisors-from-a-distance. We deploy with you. We measure outcomes. We close the engagement when the discipline is internalized, not when the contract runs out.

**HLK Tech Lab** is where research becomes software. We build internally first — every product we offer, we run on ourselves before we offer it. The tools we sell are the tools we use. MADEIRA, KiRBe, ENVOY, and the products coming next are productized from work we did for Holistika before we did it for anyone else.

The flywheel: research validates → strategy guides execution → execution validates research. Each turn produces new questions, new frameworks, new tools, and new credibility. We are a consultancy with a lab and a lab with a consultancy. Neither piece is decorative.

### What this means for you

If you are a company looking for clarity on what you actually do, how you actually do it, and where you actually leak — we are the operation that brings the discipline.

If you are a company looking to scale with EU funding, with technological component, with a credible operational story — we have built that operational story for ourselves and for the companies we have worked with.

If you are a company looking for a partner who will tell you the inconvenient evidence before they tell you the convenient strategy — we are calibrated for that conversation.

### How we got here

The founder, Fayçal Njoya, has built operations across hospitality (a fitness franchise scaled from concept to seven venues with €50K initial capital and €1.7M raised; subsequently engaged by the marketing agency that worked with the franchise to build their own internal processes), data and intelligence (Chief Information Officer / Chief Data Officer / Chief Strategist roles across multiple operating companies), and AI / agentic systems (the AKOS knowledge operating system that runs our own internal operation). The pattern: structured research, protected intelligence, audience-calibrated communication, and dogfooded technology, repeatedly producing capital-efficient outcomes that did not require the typical scale of capital deployment.

Holistika is the formalization of that pattern as a company that does it for others. We do not promise the impossible. We promise the discipline, the framework, and the operational accountability to make the possible legible.

### What we are not

We are not a generic transformation consultancy. We are not a stock-photograph-of-handshakes brand. We are not a chatbot wrapper claiming to be AI. We are not a product company larping as a consultancy or a consultancy larping as a product company.

We are an operating company that brings inside-the-company disciplines to other companies, and we operate the same disciplines on ourselves first.

## 4. Drift gate contract (P7)

`scripts/validate_brand_vision_drift.py` (created I66 P7) does the following:

1. Reads this file; extracts the prose between `<!-- public-vision:start -->` and `<!-- public-vision:end -->`.
2. Reads `boilerplate/app/vision/page.tsx`; extracts the rendered prose from MDX content.
3. Normalizes whitespace + trailing punctuation.
4. Asserts string equality after normalization.
5. On mismatch: print precise diff + exit 1.

`scripts/release-gate.py` invokes the validator before merge. CI breaks on drift.

A deliberate-drift verification is filed at I66 P7 closure as `reports/p7-drift-gate-fake-drift-demo-YYYY-MM-DD.md`: agent introduces one subtle word change in either side; CI fails as expected; agent reverts; CI passes. Operator reviews + signs the verification.
