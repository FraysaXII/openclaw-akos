# Methodology IP Minting Path

> **No trademark or filing claims without counsel.** This charter describes **how methodological work becomes governable IP artefacts** inside Holistika’s Operating System. **Commercial registration, Nice-class selection, and filing strategy** require **external legal counsel** approval before any government or registry submission. When in doubt, pause and consult Legal Counsel per [`SOP-LEGAL_IP_REGISTER_MAINTENANCE_001.md`](../../../People/Legal/SOP-LEGAL_IP_REGISTER_MAINTENANCE_001.md) and [`SOP-TRADEMARK_NAMING_GOVERNANCE_001.md`](../../../People/Legal/SOP-TRADEMARK_NAMING_GOVERNANCE_001.md).

---

## Purpose

Methodology IP spans **corpus** (research + operator practice + curriculum outputs), **distilled methods** (repeatable patterns, playbooks, KM topics), and **brand-bounded expression** (Holistika vessel vs personal-name lineage). Strand H (I73 P8) freezes a **repeatable path** so Brand, Legal, Learning, and Research do not fork parallel “IP systems.”

**Operator default (I73):** **Holistika** as the default **vessel** for business methodology IP; founder acts as **sole investor** in the bootstrapping posture; anchor themes **process engineering**, **business engineering**, and **foresight** inform what gets promoted from corpus to protectable or licensable form. **Per-asset** brand-vs-name choices stay **deferred-at-filing-time** per **D-IH-73-F**.

## Lifecycle (corpus to asset)

Generic pipeline — not every corpus row traverses every stage:

1. **Corpus capture** — working notes, engagements, curricula, diagnostics; classified by [`access_levels.md`](../../../People/Compliance/canonicals/access_levels.md) + [`confidence_levels.md`](../../../People/Compliance/canonicals/confidence_levels.md) + vault routing (see [`KB_HUMAN_READABILITY_CHARTER.md`](../../../Operations/PMO/canonicals/KB_HUMAN_READABILITY_CHARTER.md)).
2. **Distill** — extract reusable patterns → candidate methodology statements, KM topics, structured exercises (Output-1 manifests per [`HLK_KM_TOPIC_FACT_SOURCE.md`](../../../People/Compliance/canonicals/HLK_KM_TOPIC_FACT_SOURCE.md)).
3. **Revise** — advisor/revisor/editor loop (Holistika Tech Lab + external advisers per existing governance); align voice with [`BRAND_VISION.md`](BRAND_VISION.md) / [`BRAND_VOICE_FOUNDATION.md`](BRAND_VOICE_FOUNDATION.md); resolve dual-register leakage on external-boundary surfaces ([`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`](BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md)).
4. **Editorial pack** — versioned canon suitable for decks, dossiers, curriculum, internal SOP citations; changelog discipline via [`LOGIC_CHANGE_LOG.md`](../../../People/canonicals/LOGIC_CHANGE_LOG.md) (brand-as-shield / methodology evolution framing — see **BT-01** discourse there).
5. **IP / trademark checklist (when applicable)** — only after Legal flags protectability: clearance, entity / sub-mark posture, filing queue coordination with [`BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE_2026-04.md`](../../../People/Legal/canonicals/BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE_2026-04.md) + [`TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md`](../../../People/Legal/canonicals/TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md). **Consult counsel before filings** — no agent-or-operator-solo filing narrative.

## Holistika default vessel vs personal-name criteria matrix

Use this **decision-support table** at editorial freeze + again at filing time. It does **not** replace counsel sign-off.

| Criterion | Favour **Holistika / sub-mark vessel** | Favour **personal-name attribution** (still company-licensed in commercial use) |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Commercial leverage** | Method will be productized, licensed, or repeated at scale under company brand. | Attribution is primarily reputational for an individual; company use is secondary. |
| **Lineage vs transfer** | Method is **transferred** into the Branded House as company know-how. | Method is explicitly framed as **personal scholarly / artistic lineage** with limited company exclusive claim. |
| **Trademarkability (screening only)** | Distinctive programme names, course titles, or service marks fit Nice-class strategy in [`TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md`](../../../People/Legal/canonicals/TRADEMARK_FILING_STRATEGY_2026-05.md). | Better protected as authorial reputation / copyright capture; trademark may be weak or generic. |
| **Jurisdiction priority** | EUIPO / OEPM-led portfolio per [`BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE_2026-04.md`](../../../People/Legal/canonicals/BRAND_HIERARCHY_AND_TRADEMARK_SCOPE_2026-04.md). | Cross-border personal moral-right considerations — counsel review. |
| **Ethics + Learning coupling** | Curriculum and automation posture must stay aligned with [`SOP-ETHICS_LEARNING_REVIEW_001.md`](../../../People/Ethics/canonicals/SOP-ETHICS_LEARNING_REVIEW_001.md) quarterly review. | Same — no exemption; personal-name still runs through Ethics + Learning review when consumed by agents or externalized. |

**Tie-break:** Default to **Holistika vessel** for business methodology IP per **D-IH-73-F**; document explicit exceptions in initiative `decision-log.md` + Legal working papers (not in this charter body).
