# Press Kit

> Source copy for press, profiles, event bios, and short public descriptions. This is not a pitch deck; it is a fact pack.

## Company Boilerplate

Holistika Research SL is a Spanish company that designs operating systems for businesses: strategy, execution, and technology working as one discipline. Holistika combines structured research, business engineering, process engineering, and an internal technology lab to help companies make their operations legible, repeatable, and scalable.

The company operates as one branded house: Holistika is the umbrella brand; Holistika R&S, Think Big, and HLK Tech Lab are delivery modes under the same legal entity. The lab develops internal-first technology, including agentic knowledge systems and workflow tooling, to turn repeatable methods into software.

## Short Company Description

Holistika Research SL designs operating systems for companies that need strategy, execution, and technology to work as one discipline.

## What Holistika Does

- Makes operations legible before companies scale.
- Designs business and process systems that can be trained, audited, and improved.
- Builds internal-first technology where the method needs software to run.
- Transfers capability so clients are not dependent on permanent consulting support.

## Proof Points
