# Onboarding Kit

> Starter kit for collaborators, hires, and trusted contributors. It explains how to read Holistika's operating system without exposing operator-private engagement details.

## 1. First Hour

Read in this order:

1. `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md` — one legal entity, one umbrella brand, three delivery modes.
2. `BRAND_VOICE_FOUNDATION.md` — how Holistika speaks.
3. `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md` — why audience context changes the message.
4. `SERVICE_OFFERING_CATALOG.md` — what Holistika offers.
5. `FOUNDER_BIO.md` — how founder credibility is represented externally.

## 2. What We Are Building

Holistika builds operating systems for companies. That means:

- research that clarifies what is true,
- business engineering that turns the truth into a model,
- process engineering that makes the model repeatable,
- technology that turns repeatable work into systems,
- handoff that makes the client stronger after the engagement.

## 3. How To Work Here

- Write decisions down.
- Cite the canonical source.
- Separate internal preparation from external prose.
- Do not invent new IDs, roles, or brand names.
- Prefer a clear artifact to a long meeting.
- Treat every reusable output as a future template candidate.

## 4. Communication Rules

External audiences do not need internal vocabulary. Translate:

| Internal preparation concept | External phrase |
|:---|:---|
| audience reading | client context |
| structured research | discovery and evidence gathering |
| source confidence | evidence quality |
| operating signal | observation |
| private objection pattern | likely concern |

When in doubt, write as the rigorous peer who removes uncertainty without performing expertise.

## 5. First Deliverable Checklist

Before your first deliverable:

- [ ] You know which audience will read it.
- [ ] You know which canonical file governs it.
- [ ] You know whether the file is internal or external.
- [ ] You have removed private names and unnecessary internal vocabulary.
- [ ] You have added a short source note or cross-reference.
- [ ] You have asked for review when the artifact will be sent outside.

## 7. What Not To Do

- Do not tell external readers how internal research is conducted unless the source file explicitly allows it.
- Do not expose private client or collaborator details.
- Do not turn Holistika R&S, Think Big, or HLK Tech Lab into separate companies.
- Do not create a new deck, proposal, or outreach template without adding it to `BRAND_TEMPLATE_REGISTRY.md`.
- Do not use "generic startup" language: excited, disruptive, revolutionary, game-changing.

## 8. Manager Review

First deliverables are reviewed by the relevant owner:

- Brand or marketing prose: Brand Manager.
- Engagement design: Holistik Researcher.
- Legal or trademark content: Legal Counsel.
- Technical architecture: CTO or System Owner.
- Hiring/collaborator copy: Talent.
