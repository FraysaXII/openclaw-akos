## 3. Method

The work follows a simple rhythm:

1. **Discover** — confirm the operating reality and decision criteria.
2. **Shape** — map the engagement path and phase outputs.
3. **Build** — produce the artifact, system, or operating capability.
4. **Transfer** — hand over the method and train the responsible team.
5. **Close** — review outcomes, unresolved risks, and next decisions.
