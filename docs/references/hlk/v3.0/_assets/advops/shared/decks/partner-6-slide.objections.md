# Partner Deck — Objection Companion

| Objection | Response Frame | Evidence |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Are you a competitor? | Map scope explicitly; do not claim "we do everything." | Complementarity map. |
| Who owns the client? | Decide ownership before pilot. | Pilot shape slide. |
| What do we get? | Identify the client outcome that improves with both teams. | Scope comparison. |
| Can you execute reliably? | Show process, templates, and delivery governance. | Engagement playbook. |

## Operator Notes

- Partnership language must be concrete or it becomes noise.
- A small pilot is stronger than a broad alliance statement.
