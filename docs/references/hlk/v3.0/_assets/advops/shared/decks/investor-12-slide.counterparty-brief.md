# Investor Deck — Audience Brief

## Reading Lens

The investor assumes most AI companies are wrappers, most consulting companies are slide shops, and most early-stage founders overstate capacity.

## Decision Criteria

- Founder credibility.
- Market thesis.
- Moat.
- Capital efficiency.
- Product path.
- Evidence quality.

## First Doubt Trigger

A claim without a number, a number without a date, or a methodology without a track record.

## Prep Checklist

- [ ] Track-record block is anonymized.
- [ ] Ask is explicit.
- [ ] Product stack is concrete.
- [ ] Service-to-software loop is easy to explain in under 90 seconds.
