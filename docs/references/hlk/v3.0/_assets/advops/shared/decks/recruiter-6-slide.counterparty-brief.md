# Recruiter Deck — Audience Brief

## Reading Lens

The recruiter or candidate assumes early-stage companies are vague about role, ownership, compensation, and operating discipline.

## Decision Criteria

- Clear role scope.
- First 90-day outcomes.
- Compensation band or decision timing.
- Evidence that work is serious and reusable.

## First Doubt Trigger

"We're flexible on compensation" without a number or date, or generic startup culture language.

## Prep Checklist

- [ ] Role scope written.
- [ ] 90-day outcomes written.
- [ ] Compensation answer prepared.
- [ ] Onboarding kit link ready.
