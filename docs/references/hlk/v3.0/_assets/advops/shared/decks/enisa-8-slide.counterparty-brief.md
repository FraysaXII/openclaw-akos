# ENISA Deck — Audience Brief

## Reading Lens

The reviewer tests whether the applicant has real technological substance, scalability, local impact, and capital discipline.

## Decision Criteria

- Technological component.
- Scalability path.
- Employment plan.
- Capital efficiency evidence.
- IP and brand protection.

## First Doubt Trigger

An AI claim without architecture, or a funding story without Spain-specific benefit.

## Prep Checklist

- [ ] Technical architecture support ready.
- [ ] Trademark filing strategy ready.
- [ ] Founder track record anonymized.
- [ ] Funding use tied to employment and product development.
