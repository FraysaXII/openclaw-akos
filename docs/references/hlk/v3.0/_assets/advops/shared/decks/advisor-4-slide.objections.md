# Advisor Deck — Objection Companion

| Objection | Response Frame | Evidence |
|:---|:---|:---|
| What do you need from me? | One precise decision, not general guidance. | Slide 02. |
| Will my advice be used? | Capture advice into one decision memo and owner. | Slide 04 closure promise. |
