# Recruiter / Hiring Deck — 6 Slides

## 01. What Holistika Is

A company building operating systems for businesses: research, execution, and technology in one discipline.

## 02. How Work Happens

Clear canon, reusable templates, explicit decisions, technical depth, and disciplined handoff.

## 03. Who Fits

People who write clearly, own systems, handle ambiguity, and want their work to become reusable infrastructure.

## 04. First 90 Days

Month 1: read canon and ship one small artifact.  
Month 2: own one reusable process or template.  
Month 3: improve a system other people depend on.

## 05. What We Avoid

Generic startup noise, unclear ownership, performative speed, and work that cannot be reused.

## 06. Next Step

Structured interview around one real artifact, one role scope, and one 90-day outcome.
