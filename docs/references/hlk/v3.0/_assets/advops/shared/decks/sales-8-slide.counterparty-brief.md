# Sales Deck — Audience Brief

## Reading Lens

The client expects expensive PowerPoint, vague pricing, and weak handoff unless proven otherwise.

## Decision Criteria

- Clear outcome.
- Price predictability.
- Confidentiality.
- Time-to-value.
- Evidence of practical competence.

## First Doubt Trigger

An English-only response to a Spanish conversation, or a price answer that sounds open-ended.

## Prep Checklist

- [ ] One concrete operating problem selected.
- [ ] Scope-in and scope-out ready.
- [ ] NDA/DPA path clear.
- [ ] Handoff promise is specific.
