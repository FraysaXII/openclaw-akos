## 3. Pause-point outcomes

### Pause-point #1 (P13.2): GOI/POI class-enum reconciliation

**Question.** Are `collaborator` and `partner` real semantic siblings or accidental duplicates?

**Decision (D-W13-C).** Real siblings; maturity-ramp distinction encoded in SOP §4.10. `collaborator` = informal + single-project + no co-branding + no maintenance commitment (all four). `partner` = strategic + contractual + co-branded + maintenance-bearing (at least three). Ramp is one-directional: `collaborator` → `partner` on conditions strengthening; no demotion path. Validator (`validate_goipoi_register.py`) continues to accept both enum values; operator discipline at SOP-level is the authoritative gate. Canonical example documented: `GOI-PRT-EFA-2026` (EFA Académie) → `partner`.

**Why no schema mutation.** The boundary is doctrinal, not enum-distinguishable. Encoding maturity in two separate enum values risks brittleness (rows would migrate as relationships mature; ref_ids would need to be either recycled or doubled). The maturity-ramp pattern keeps row identity stable while reflecting the relationship's evolution in the `class` cell.

### Pause-point #2 (P13.4): Canonical-CSV tranche

**Sub-decisions.**

- **D-W13-D — `related_party` column.** New boolean column added to `GOI_POI_REGISTER.csv` with enum `{'', 'true', 'false'}`. Empty default is backwards-compatible for every Initiative 21 / 22 / 24 / 31 row. Marks engagements with related entities (family members, board members, advisors-with-equity, sister's business) for SOC disclosure norms.
- **D-W13-E — Trainee role placement.** New row `Holistik Researcher Trainee` added to `baseline_organisation.csv` under `Research` area; access_level=2; rates 25/35/50; reports_to=`Holistik Researcher`. Trainees inherit `area`/`entity` parent context but carry a distinct access-level posture (2 vs the senior role's 4) and a distinct rate band.

**Sub-decision out of scope (deferred).**

- The `Holistik Researcher` rate band itself was not edited; rates were inherited unchanged from the existing row.
- The decision to promote `_engagement-template/` skeletons from `_candidate` to `active` engagements (i.e., the threshold for "this is a real engagement") remains operator discretion per the `customer-engagements-2026.md` pointer.
