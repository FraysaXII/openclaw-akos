## 1. Why this dig

The SUEZ engagement, the Asesoría Hostelería pre-engagement, the EFA partner collaboration, the ShadowGPU collaborator inbound, and the founder-incorporation adviser engagements — read together — surface seven gaps that the P13 workspace blueprint did not close:

1. **Federal canonicals not yet authored at owning area-role homes** (D-IH-70-A; ratified 2026-05-12). All canonical CSVs and SOPs still live under `docs/references/hlk/compliance/` or scattered top-level `Admin/O5-1/<area>/<role>/` paths. The role-owner-canonical contract from P13.1 needs the federated home + master `CANONICAL_REGISTRY.csv`.
2. **Engagement registry not yet built** (D-IH-70-F). Five-plus engagement counterparties exist (SUEZ, Asesoría, EFA, ShadowGPU, Bâtard, future EnviniAge, future advisers) but no canonical `ENGAGEMENT_REGISTRY.csv` + Supabase mirror + ERP panel slot.
3. **Brand sub-discipline ontology empty** (Phase 5). `Marketing/Brand/AV/`, `Copywriter/`, `Design/`, `UX Designer/` are present as scaffolds (gitkeep-only) but no charters, no SOPs, no Impeccable-for-copywriting canonical.
4. **Multilingual + counterparty-README contract unowned** (Phase 7). FR / ES / EN engagement surfaces exist, but no doctrine for bilingual-files (`README.fr.md` + `README.en.md` + 5-line pointer `README.md`), no per-language validators, no four-surface matrix (engagement README + counterparty README + cover-pack + customer pack).
5. **Founder methodology v0 to v3.0 lineage uncodified** (Phase 9). `FOUNDER_TRAJECTORY_INTERNAL.md` exists; `FOUNDER_BIO.md` exists; but the *versioned methodology* (v0 to v3.0 with breakthrough-driven re-versioning) is implicit, not canonical.
6. **Personal-knowledge-corpus governance absent** (Phase 9 §9.6). Notebooks, self-audios, R&L spreadsheets, CVs, epistemology images live across `temp-move-or-delete-hlk-business-context/` (untracked) and the operator's local OS — but no `FOUNDER_CORPUS_INVENTORY.md` + retention-class + confidentiality matrix.
7. **Research as new top-level area** (Phase 4.7 + Conundrum 11 ratified 2026-05-12). Existing technique folders under `Admin/O5-1/Research/*` (Methodology Pillars / HUMINT / OSINT / Deep Research / Intelligence Matrix) are scattered; no Research-area-as-discipline parent (Methodology / Intelligence / Diagnosis / Validation).

I70 lands all seven gaps + the architectural backbone (federal canonicals + ERP architecture + MADEIRA-AKOS reserved + Marketing M3 redesign + People restructure + SMO active charter), in 17 phases over ~39-46 calendar days.
