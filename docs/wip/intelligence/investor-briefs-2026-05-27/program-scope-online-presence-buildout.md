# Holistika online-presence build-out — 2026 Q3-Q4 scope

> Two-page companion to `brief-05-online-presence.md`. Names the
> programs of work that lift Holistika's public surface to the level
> a sophisticated B2B research investor would expect, on a defined
> timeline and with explicit non-goals.

## 1. Scope and non-scope

**In scope** (Q3-Q4 2026):

- Website expansion (`holistikaresearch.com`): new research-output
  index, new founder + team page, new "What we work on" engagement
  index.
- LinkedIn cadence: 3 short essays per week from the founder, 1
  longer-form research piece per month. Topics drawn from real
  engagement work and from the firm's source ledger.
- Case-study pattern: every closed engagement (when signed and
  closed in the future) generates a public case study within thirty
  days of close, authored against the brand baseline reality matrix.
- Public methodology index: a public listing of the fourteen
  specialty-discipline modules, with one-paragraph summary each and
  links to the doctrines where appropriate.

**Explicitly NOT in scope** (Q3-Q4 2026):

- No paid media spend, no ad campaigns, no growth-hacking pipeline.
- No press strategy beyond responding to earned-media requests when
  they arrive.
- No fundraise-stage announcements or pitch-deck publication.
- No third-party content licensing or guest-author programs.

## 2. Why this shape

Holistika's positioning depends on **earned credibility from depth**,
not on **paid amplification from volume**. The C1 prong-d research
on early-stage founder online presence confirmed what was already
the firm's instinct: in B2B research, what investors and customers
read on LinkedIn and on the founder's public profile drives more
inbound than paid traffic. Volume without substance erodes the
position; substance with cadence builds it.

The cadence above (3 short + 1 long per week-month) is the threshold
research suggests is sustainable for a single founder while still
being visible enough to register on an investor's radar between
calls.

### 3.1 Website expansion

| Surface | Owner | ETA | Acceptance |

### 3.2 LinkedIn cadence

| Surface | Owner | Cadence | Acceptance |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Short essay | Founder/CEO + Storytelling Manager | 3 per week | Each essay translates one internal-register insight into the external register per the brand baseline reality matrix. |
| Long-form research | Founder/CEO + Research Head | 1 per month | Each piece cites at least one Tier-1 source from the C1 source ledger and at least one internal precedent. |
| Engagement reply | Resonance Manager (founder-held until role activates) | Within 48h per the LinkedIn DM channel doctrine | Per `LINKEDIN_DM_DOCTRINE.md` §6 cadence rules. |

### 3.3 Case-study pattern

When an engagement closes (future):

1. The closure-UAT report for the engagement triggers a case-study
   authoring task within five business days.
2. The case study is authored against the same dual-register contract
   used for the engagement deliverables.
3. The case study is published on the website within thirty days of
   the engagement close.
4. A render-trail manifest sidecar is captured per the external-render
   discipline.

The first case studies are expected in 2027 when current live
conversations (SUEZ, Websitz) move to signed and closed status.
Before then, the surface stays honest: no synthetic case studies
authored from un-signed work.

### 3.4 Public methodology index

By Q4 2026, the website carries a public-grade index of the fourteen
specialty discipline modules currently in the operating system. Each
row carries: discipline name, one-paragraph summary in translated
register, status (active / charter), and a link to the public-grade
doctrine where appropriate.

This is the public-facing answer to "what is Holistika's moat?" — the
methodology itself, indexed transparently, without giving away the
working ledgers.

## 4. Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Cadence proves unsustainable for solo founder | Medium | Storytelling Manager role activates per the M3 propagation matrix when first signed engagement closes; absorbs LinkedIn cadence load. |
| Public surfaces drift from internal register | Low | Brand drift gate runs in CI; external-render trail captures sha256 manifests on every public render. |
| Case-study pipeline empty into 2027 | Medium | Companion plan to surface methodology-stack outputs (specialty mints, doctrine releases) as public-grade research artifacts so the publication pipeline is not gated entirely on signed engagements. |
| Press coverage arrives before the firm is ready | Low | Earned-only posture; founder declines press requests that exceed current scope. |

## 5. What we will report on at the next check-in

At a 90-day check-in (target: Q4 2026 mid-quarter), Holistika will
share:

- LinkedIn cadence achieved (essays per week, research pieces per
  month) and what was published.
- First case-study pipeline status when conversations move toward
  close.
- Public methodology index progress.

The report will use the same brand register the rest of the firm's
external surfaces use. No spin.
