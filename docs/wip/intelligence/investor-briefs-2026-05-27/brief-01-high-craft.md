# Holistika for the high-craft investor

> Single-page brief for an investor who reads technique closely and
> wants to see depth before price. Read time: 3 minutes.

## What this is

Holistika is a research organization that has been building, in code
and in writing, a complete methodology for how a small consulting firm
runs itself — process governance, quality discipline, brand register,
engagement economics, client delivery, agentic collaboration. Most of
this lives as a fourteen-specialty fabric of doctrines, each one paired
with a validator and a runbook so the discipline is enforced
mechanically and not just spoken about.

The work is not a slide deck. It is a working code base, a versioned
vault of canonical documents, and an internal operating system the
founder uses every day to run client engagements.

## Why this might interest you

If you fund teams whose method is the moat, the question you usually
ask first is "how do they actually operate?". Holistika answers that
question by showing you the operating system.

- Fourteen quality-discipline modules are active or in trial promotion
  today, each with a published doctrine, a Pydantic-validated runbook,
  and a paired SOP. The most recent two (collaborator-share governance
  and the synthesis-before-tranche pre-commit gate) were minted and
  ratified in the past three weeks under a real client engagement.
- Engagement economics — how Holistika splits revenue with delivery
  partners on a single deal — is governed as code, not as a side
  agreement on email. Four base patterns plus one stackable overlay
  cover every commercial shape the firm has encountered, and a
  validator refuses commits that would produce silent margin drift.
- The brand register is split into an internal vocabulary used by
  agents and operators, and an external vocabulary used in everything
  a client or investor reads. A drift gate fails the build if the
  internal vocabulary leaks outward.

This is what discipline-as-infrastructure looks like when a small team
takes the engineering view seriously instead of dressing up consulting
slides.

## What Holistika is today (no spin)

- A research methodology, fully documented and version-controlled, at
  version 3.2 of the operating doctrine.
- Two products in active development: KiRBe, a knowledge platform
  designed for sophisticated B2B research workflows; and MADEIRA, an
  agent layer that consumes the same operating doctrine the founder
  uses, so human and agent operate against the same contract.
- Two live commercial conversations underway: SUEZ Group (B2B Énergie
  Suez Webuy), where a co-branded proposal and a customer-facing
  customer-pack have been authored and exchanged; and Websitz/Rushly,
  the worked example for the deep-partner share pattern. Neither is a
  signed engagement at the date of this brief.
- A founding team of one (Founder/CEO) with MADEIRA as the agent in
  charge. The methodology is what scales the founder, not headcount.

## The asks

What would be useful in a first conversation with you, in order of
realism:

1. **A 45-minute call** for you to inspect the operating substrate.
   The founder will walk through one engagement end-to-end — proposal
   authored, share pattern recorded, settlement computed, render
   trail captured — using the actual repository, not a deck.
2. **A second-round walk of one specialty in depth.** Pick any of the
   fourteen specialties; the founder will show you the doctrine, the
   chassis, the validator, the tests, the SOP, the cursor rule, and
   the worked example.
3. **A note from you on what would change your view** of methodology
   as a fundable wedge in 2026 B2B research and consulting.

Holistika is not currently raising on a closed round. If you want to
move toward a position, the founder would prefer a conversation about
fit and method first; commercial structure follows.
