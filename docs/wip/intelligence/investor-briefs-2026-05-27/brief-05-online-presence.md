# Holistika — what is online today, what is coming

> Single-page brief for an investor who evaluates firms by what they
> can find online before the first call. Read time: 3 minutes.
> Companion: `program-scope-online-presence-buildout.md` for the
> mid-term build plan.

## What this is

A short, honest map of Holistika's current online presence and the
plan to expand it. If you fund founders by what their public footprint
shows about how they think, this brief points you at what to read and
tells you what is intentionally not there yet.

## What you will find online today (no spin)

- **Public website**: `holistikaresearch.com`. Sets the research-firm
  positioning, names the firm's stance, points to the founder. Active
  and maintained.
- **Sub-mark public sites**: `kirbe.com` (product positioning, under
  active build) and other sub-marks under the Holistika Brand House.
- **Founder profile**: a single named founder of record on LinkedIn
  with a research-positioning bio aligned to the website.
- **Public repositories**: parts of the methodology stack are
  open-source-shaped (the operating doctrine is publicly cited from
  the website where commercially appropriate); the working repository
  itself is private during the current research-density phase.

## What is intentionally NOT online yet

- **No high-frequency content engine**. Holistika is not posting
  3-5 LinkedIn essays a week or running a podcast. Content cadence
  is one published research artifact per month or per quarter,
  authored from real engagement work rather than from a content
  calendar.
- **No public investor materials**. Pitch decks, full team pages,
  and roadmap collateral are reserved for 1:1 conversations and are
  produced from the same operating system that produces customer
  deliverables.
- **No press strategy yet**. The firm has not pursued press coverage;
  earned credibility comes from engagement work and from the
  methodology itself.

This is a deliberate posture: until the engagement substrate is
deep enough to ground content claims, Holistika prefers a quieter
public surface to a high-volume one that would outrun the work.

## What is coming next

A defined online-presence program is scoped to lift the public
surface to the level a sophisticated B2B research investor would
expect to see before a serious second conversation. The companion
document (`program-scope-online-presence-buildout.md`) covers:

- Cadence model (one durable research artifact per month, three
  shorter LinkedIn essays per week translating internal work into
  external register).
- Team and About pages (founder-level transparency; agent-in-charge
  named explicitly; methodology stack publicly indexed).
- Case-study pattern (every closed engagement produces a public
  case study within thirty days of close, governed by the same
  external-render discipline as customer deliverables).
- Press posture (earned media only, triggered by engagement results,
  never by funding-stage announcements).

## What Holistika is today (no spin)

A research organization run by the Founder/CEO with MADEIRA as agent
in charge. Fourteen quality-discipline methodology modules in the
operating system. Two products in active development (KiRBe, MADEIRA).
Two live commercial conversations underway (SUEZ Group, Websitz/Rushly).
No signed engagements at the date of this brief.

## The asks

1. **A 30-minute call** to walk through the online-presence program
   above and pressure-test the cadence against what you would expect
   to see by Q1 2027.
2. **A signal** from you on which online surface you read most
   carefully when evaluating a firm — site, LinkedIn, press, public
   repos — so the program ordering can favor that surface.
3. **Optional**: a 90-day check-in to see the new surfaces live.

## Where to find more

- Public website: `holistikaresearch.com`.
- Public sub-mark sites: `kirbe.com` and others under Holistika Brand
  House.
- Companion artifact: `program-scope-online-presence-buildout.md` (in

A quiet public surface that becomes a deep one — on schedule — is
worth more than a loud one that does not survive a second look.
