# Holistika — operational discipline you can rely on

> Single-page brief for an investor who values operational
> predictability and clear scope above novelty or depth-on-craft.
> Read time: 3 minutes.

## What this is

Holistika is a small research organization run by a single founder
with an agent in charge as collaborator. The firm publishes its scope
clearly, refuses work outside that scope, runs every deliverable
through a mechanical quality bar before it leaves, and keeps the
operating system the same week to week so partners and customers know
what they get.

This brief stays at the level of operational fact. It does not
oversell the methodology and it does not promise anything outside the
firm's defined scope.

## What Holistika does

Holistika produces research-grade methodology and tooling for
sophisticated B2B engagements. Concretely:

- **Engagement work**: scoped consulting and research engagements,
  authored end-to-end with a documented governance lifecycle (proposal
  through settlement) and a fixed commercial scope per contract.
- **Product work**: KiRBe (a knowledge platform under active
  development) and MADEIRA (the agent layer, under active
  development). Neither product is on sale today.

What Holistika does NOT do:

- Bespoke build at customer-IT scale (the firm authors specifications
  the customer's IT team implements).
- Generalist consulting outside the published scope (research,
  branding, methodology, agentic operations).
- Rapid pivots into adjacent verticals without a documented research
  pass first.

## How the firm operates

- Single founder of record (Founder/CEO). MADEIRA, the agent in
  charge, executes against the same operating doctrine. No hidden
  team layers.
- Every deliverable goes through a synthesis-before-tranche check
  before commit and a closing-loop verification after. The mechanical
  bar is published and consistent across engagements.
- Engagement economics are governed as code, not as ad-hoc side
  letters. The shared methodology covers four base commercial shapes
  (direct consultancy, BD-intro only, joint venture, deep partner)
  plus one stackable BD-commission overlay. Settlement math is
  reproducible and auditable.
- External communication uses an explicit translated register so
  internal vocabulary never leaks into customer-facing prose. A drift
  gate fails the build if it does.

## Live conversations today

Two live commercial conversations are underway:

- **SUEZ Group Énergie Suez Webuy**: a documented proposal and a
  five-document customer pack have been authored and exchanged.
  Status: live conversation, not yet signed.
- **Websitz/Rushly**: the worked example for the firm's deep-partner
  share pattern. Status: live conversation.

Neither carries a signed purchase order at the date of this brief.
Holistika reports its pipeline state truthfully.

## Why this might interest you

If you fund teams whose competence is in **showing up the same way
every week** and **delivering inside the scope they sold**, Holistika
is a calm option. The firm will not pitch you on a roadmap that
exceeds its capacity, and it will not stretch the truth on what is
signed.

If you want adrenaline, this is not the brief for you.

## The asks

1. **A 30-minute call** for a straightforward conversation on scope,
   pipeline, and operational rhythm.
2. **A clear signal** from you on what fundability bar matters most
   in your reading — predictable cash, signed pipeline, or operational
   discipline.
3. **Optional**: a quarterly update cadence rather than constant
   contact, if a fit emerges.
