## 2. Definitional regression — "What is an information asset for Holistika?"

**Current (implicit):** any row in a governed CSV/canonical that other areas consume, maintained under PRECEDENCE + process_list pairing.

**Regression test:** survives vault mass (`SRC-INF-INT-*` CORPINT) and DCAM/Laney OSINT — **partially**:

- ✓ **Inventory:** contract registries, FINOPS registers, adapter registries, intelligence/radar registers.
- ✗ **Valuation:** no register records economic value, carrying cost, or depreciation of stale mirrors.
- ✗ **Incentives:** collaborator share (`SRC-INF-INT-004`) prices partner splits but not internal build/maintain tradeoffs for data products.

**Research-grounded working definition (for P5 ratification):**

> A Holistika **information asset** is a governed artifact (register row, canonical, mirror table, or research pack) with **named consumers**, **maintenance owner**, **verify/mirror cost**, and **trust grade** — whose economic outcome is measurable as *rework avoided*, *revenue enabled*, *risk reduced*, or *explicit opex*.
