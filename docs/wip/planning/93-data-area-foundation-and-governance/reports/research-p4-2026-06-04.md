# I93 P4 — research packet: semantic/metrics layer + formal lineage

> §9.0 research bar for `SEMANTIC_LAYER.md`, `METRICS_REGISTRY.csv`, and
> `SOP-DATA_LINEAGE_001.md`. Pairs `thi_data_dtp_31` (KPI/reporting catalog) and
> `thi_data_dtp_275` (formal lineage).

## 0. Load-bearing finding

Holistika already **defines metrics implicitly** (DataOps DATA-07, area-completeness
score, capability counts) but lacks a **named semantic layer** — a registry where
business meaning, grain, owners, and source contracts are declared once and consumed
by dashboards, MCP tools, and agents. Lineage is **partially automated** via
`sync_hlk_neo4j.py` (T3 projection) but not operationalised as a steward process.

P4 names both: **METRICS_REGISTRY** as the define-once catalog; **lineage SOP +
data_lineage_check.py** as the T1→T2→T3 evidence runbook paired to `thi_data_dtp_275`.

## 2. External research sweep

### 2.1 Semantic layer (dbt / AtScale class)

**Define-once-use-everywhere:** metric definitions carry business owner, technical
owner, grain, and dimensions; consumers reference metric_id not ad-hoc SQL.
Holistika on-ramp: CSV registry + Pydantic (same pattern as contract registry).

### 2.2 OpenLineage

OpenLineage standardises **job/dataset/run** lineage events. Holistika posture:
Neo4j graph + vault markdown LINKS_TO edges are the **read index**; full OpenLineage
emitter forward-charter at P6+ when KiRBe ingest emits events. P4 documents the
manual+automated hybrid path.

### 2.3 DAMA metadata + quality linkage

DAMA Ch.10 (Metadata) + Ch.11 (Quality): metrics must link to quality rules and
source systems. Our `source_contract_id` → `DATA_CONTRACT_REGISTRY` implements this.
