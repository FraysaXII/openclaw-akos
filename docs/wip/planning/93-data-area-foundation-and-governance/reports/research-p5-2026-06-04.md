## 0. Load-bearing finding

Holistika already **stores party master data** in `GOI_POI_REGISTER.csv` with stable
`ref_id` handles and off-repo redaction for real names (`POL-PII-GOIPOI-DISPLAY-NAME-OBFUSCATION`),
but lacks an **operational MDM SOP** — match/merge rules, golden-record ownership, and a
steward cadence. Privacy/retention rules are **scattered** across `POLICY_REGISTER.csv`
(PII scope + dossier retention) without a **DATA-area classification lattice** that
feeds `DATA_CONTRACT_REGISTRY.classification` and `COMPONENT_SERVICE_MATRIX.data_classification`.

P5 closes both gaps and resolves the **BI/warehouse non-goal**: charter lightly vs explicit
"not now" per roadmap scope control (no warehouse build in I93).

## 2. External research sweep

### 2.1 DAMA Reference & Master Data (Ch.8)

Golden-record stewardship requires: **identify authoritative source**, **match/merge rules**,
**data steward accountability**, and **consumer notification** on golden-record changes.
Holistika on-ramp: git-canonical `GOI_POI_REGISTER` + quarterly steward review paired to
`thi_data_dtp_32`; full MDM tooling (Reltio/Semarchy class) forward-charter.

### 2.2 DAMA Data Security / Privacy (Ch.5)

Classification lattice must be **small, enumerable, and mappable** to access levels and
contract surfaces. Holistika five-tier enum: `public | community | internal | confidential | restricted`.

### 2.3 GDPR storage limitation + legal hold

Retention schedules declare **default periods** + **legal-hold override** (restricted +
`legal_hold=true` on component matrix). Erasure requests route to Legal + Compliance;
git history immutability acknowledged — operational mirrors trimmed per schedule.
