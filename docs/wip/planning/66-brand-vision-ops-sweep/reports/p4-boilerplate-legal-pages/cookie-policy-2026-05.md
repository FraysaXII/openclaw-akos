## Cookie Policy

**Last updated**: `[YYYY-MM-DD]`

This Cookie Policy explains how **Holistika Research SL** ("**Holistika**", "**we**") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at `https://holistikaresearch.com` and its subdomains (the "**Site**"). It supplements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

### 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device and remember information about your visit (e.g., language preference). Similar technologies include local storage and pixel tags.

### 2. Categories of cookies we use

We use four categories of cookies, classified per the EDPB Cookie Guidelines and Spanish AEPD guidance:

| Category | Purpose | Consent required? | Examples |
|:---|:---|:---:|:---|
| **Strictly necessary** | Essential for Site to function (session management, security, load balancing) | No (Art. 5(3) ePrivacy exemption) | Session cookies, CSRF tokens, locale-preference cookies |
| **Performance / analytics** | Aggregate usage analytics (page views, time on page) — **anonymised** | **Yes** (consent before placing) | `[Privacy-respecting analytics provider, e.g., Plausible, Fathom, or self-hosted]` |
| **Functionality** | Remember your preferences (language, accessibility settings) | **Yes** if non-essential | Language preference if not session-based |
| **Marketing / advertising** | We do **not** use marketing or advertising cookies. The Site is informational. | N/A — none used | None |

### 4. How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies through:

- **Our cookie-consent banner** (shown on first visit; accessible later via "Cookie preferences" in the footer).
- **Your browser settings** (most browsers allow you to refuse, accept, or delete cookies).
- **Browser extensions** (privacy-focused extensions like uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger).

Refusing analytics or functionality cookies may affect your experience but does not prevent you from using the Site.

### 5. Third parties

When we use third-party services that set cookies (e.g., the analytics provider listed above), those services are bound by Data Processing Agreements with us per GDPR Article 28. The third-party provider's privacy policy applies to their processing of your data; links are listed in the cookie-consent banner.

### 6. International transfers

Some cookie providers may transfer data outside the EEA. We rely on the same transfer mechanisms documented in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) §5.

### 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes (e.g., a new cookie provider) require renewed consent through the cookie-consent banner.

### 8. Contact

For questions about cookies, contact `privacy@holistikaresearch.com`.
