# I66 Audience Journeys

7 audiences × landing context × first-thought × bridge-frame × decision-criteria × evidence-types-trusted × first-doubt-trigger × the artifact-set I66 produces for them.

This document is the operational input to `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md` (P1). When the matrix is canonicalized, it absorbs these journeys; this file remains the I66 charter snapshot of why we wrote what we wrote.

## J-OP — Operator (founder + future BrandOps Lead)

- **Lands at.** AKOS workspace; cursor + MCP agents producing brand outputs; `/governance` panel in hlk-erp; quarterly canon review.
- **First thought.** "Are agents producing on-canon prose? Where's drift? What needs rotation?"
- **Bridge frame.** Internal — full HUMINT vocabulary OK; baseline-reality matrix readable as "intelligence tradecraft + dual register"; founder background known.
- **Decision criteria.** Drift gates green? Quarterly canon refresh on schedule? OPS_REGISTER trademark + filings in flight? Engagement intelligence reports filed?
- **Evidence trusted.** Numbers from `governance.brand_template_registry_view`; cycle closeout reports; CI signal from drift gates; transcript-pattern evidence in voice canonicals.
- **First doubt.** "Is this self-checkpoint discipline real or theater? Are agents reading the matrix or just citing it?"
- **I66 produces for them.** All canonicals + 4 drift gates + 2 cursor rules + agent-checkpoint discipline + `/governance/brand-templates` + `/governance/intelligence` panels + cycle closeout report. P8 UAT walkthrough is the operator's deepest pass; pause records signed at every transition.

## J-IN — Investor (seed / pre-seed; ENISA / public funds; angel)

- **Lands at.** `/manifiesto/holistika`, `/vision`, investor deck, founder bio, dossier.
- **First thought.** "Is this a real company with a real founder, real customers, real defensible something?"
- **Bridge frame.** External — research-grade rigor + execution capacity + capital efficiency. NOT "we use intelligence tradecraft." Yes "we use structured research methodology." Yes "we have a track record of capital-efficient execution."
- **Decision criteria.** Founder credibility + market thesis + traction + moat. Specifically for ENISA-style EU public funds: technological component, scalability, employment generation.
- **Evidence trusted.** Anonymized methodology track record (50K€ → 7 venues, ~1.7M raised); the ENISA-specific deck; verifiable references on NDA; founder bio long-form; dossier figures aligned with public manifesto + vision page; investor deck slide 9 ("Why Holistika now").
- **First doubt.** "Is this another consulting shop pretending to be a SaaS?" → addressed by lab-channel pipeline (research validates → strategy guides → execution scales) + concrete product-stack diagram (MADEIRA + KiRBe + ENVOY).
- **I66 produces for them.** Investor 12-slide deck + `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md` companion (operator pre-meeting prep). FOUNDER_BIO.md long-form + per-audience FAQ. PRESS_KIT.md with verifiable references on NDA. Public `/vision` page coherent with deck. Trademark filings in progress (signal: not pre-revenue toy; legitimate IP-protected stack).

## J-AD — Advisor (strategic; legal; technical; financial)

- **Lands at.** `/manifiesto/holistika`, advisor 4-slide deck, ADVOPS engagement materials (per `akos-adviser-engagement.mdc`), engagement playbook.
- **First thought.** "What does this team actually need from me? Are they organized enough to take advice and act on it?"
- **Bridge frame.** External — light professional. Advisors care about utility-of-time more than hype. Brevity > depth on the first read.
- **Decision criteria.** Clear ask + clear scope + clear closure rhythm + responsiveness signal (filed instruments register; advisor-questions register).
- **Evidence trusted.** Advisor 4-slide deck (concise); `/governance/intelligence` panel (signal of professional discipline); ADVOPS register completeness (per existing rule); engagement playbook readability.
- **First doubt.** "Is this signal-rich or signal-noise?" → addressed by advisor-deck brevity + filed-instruments register completeness.
- **I66 produces for them.** Advisor 4-slide deck + `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md` (operator preps which questions to expect from this advisor's domain). ENGAGEMENT_PLAYBOOK.md. Cross-reference to ADVOPS canonicals already governed by `akos-adviser-engagement.mdc`.

## J-ENISA — ENISA application reviewer (public-funds technical evaluation panel)

- **Lands at.** ENISA application packet (constructed from artifacts shipped here); ENISA-specific deck (8-slide, P6).
- **First thought.** "Is this a fundable technology project? Can they execute? Will it generate Spain-economy employment + technological sovereignty signal?"
- **Bridge frame.** External + Spain-specific + EU-funding-specific. Vocabulary is formal; structure is rigorous; numbers are verified; cite ENISA criteria explicitly.
- **Decision criteria.** Technological component (AI / agentic / data); scalability (lab → channel pipeline + product stack); employment (3 sub-mark Lead roles + ~20 part-time collaborators); market traction; capital efficiency (anonymized track record).
- **Evidence trusted.** Spain-flagged corporate posture (Holistika Research SL, Madrid + Madeira presence); ENISA deck-specific structure; product-stack technical depth (MADEIRA Agent 5-agent coordination; KiRBe KM platform; ENVOY channel adapter); founder anonymized track record.
- **First doubt.** "Is this enough technological substance for a tech-fund vs a consulting-fund?" → addressed by lab-as-credibility narrative + product-stack depth + founder's CIO/CDO-level track record.
- **I66 produces for them.** ENISA 8-slide deck + `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md` (operator preps for technical-panel objections). FOUNDER_BIO.md medium-form. Trademark filings as IP-protection signal. ARCHITECTURE.md updated with Branded House + lab-channel pipeline diagram. Cross-references to ADVOPS Ayuda-T Pymes incorporation work.

## J-PT — Partner (strategic alliance; technology partner; channel partner)

- **Lands at.** `/services`, `/tech-lab`, partner 4-slide deck (P6).
- **First thought.** "Where do they fit in my ecosystem? What's the joint-value-prop? Are they likely to compete or complement?"
- **Bridge frame.** External; partnership-economics-focused; joint-value clarity > individual-strength.
- **Decision criteria.** Clear scope of complementary services (no overlap with partner's offering); operational maturity (reliable delivery); joint-go-to-market rhythm (cadence + materials).
- **Evidence trusted.** SERVICE_OFFERING_CATALOG (6 services × 3 arms × 3 tiers) — partner can map their offerings into the matrix; partner deck explicit "what we do alone, what we do with you, what you do alone." `/services` page same matrix in public form.
- **First doubt.** "Is this competitor-disguised-as-partner?" → addressed by service catalog clarity + scope-of-complementary-services articulation.
- **I66 produces for them.** Partner 4-slide deck + `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md`. SERVICE_OFFERING_CATALOG. `/services` rewrite. Engagement playbook section on partner-engagement.

## J-RC — Recruiter / hire / part-time collaborator

- **Lands at.** ONBOARDING_KIT.md (post-hire); recruiter 6-slide deck (pre-hire); onboarding kit + brand digest.
- **First thought.** "Is this a serious place to work? Will my time produce work I'm proud of? How clear is the leadership + scope?"
- **Bridge frame.** External; talent-funnel; emphasis on career-density (what you'll learn / build / ship).
- **Decision criteria.** Clear sub-mark Lead structure (Holistika R&S + Think Big + HLK Tech Lab); operational discipline visible (canonicals, drift gates, cursor rules); compensation + scope clarity; canon onboarding rigor (the existence of `BRAND_DIGEST` for new hires is itself a signal).
- **Evidence trusted.** Recruiter deck; ONBOARDING_KIT visible in repo; `governance` panel public-screenshot signal; FOUNDER_BIO.md short-form.
- **First doubt.** "Will I have agency or will I be a copy-paste agent?" → addressed by sub-mark Lead role definitions + collaborator-driven SOPs (e.g., who maintains BRAND_SPANISH_PATTERNS) + "we hire structured-research operators not copy-paste contractors" voice in recruiter deck.
- **I66 produces for them.** Recruiter 6-slide deck + companions. ONBOARDING_KIT.md (welcome + canonicals tour + Cursor / MCP setup + first-week SOP exposure). FOUNDER_BIO.md short-form. SOP-AGENT_BRAND_DIGEST_001 (which describes how new agents + new humans onboard to brand canon).

## J-CU — Customer (SME owner; entrepreneur with EU-funding ambitions; mid-market operator)

- **Lands at.** `/services`, `/manifiesto/holistika`, contact form, then sales-flow (welcome email → discovery interview → proposal → contract).
- **First thought.** "Is this consulting that delivers something real, or expensive PowerPoint-as-a-service? Are they professional? Will they protect my data?"
- **Bridge frame.** External + customer-specific (per `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX` row "customer-SME"). Tone: "professional like a big company acting on behalf of SMEs." Vocabulary: "discovery interview" not "elicitation"; "research" not "intelligence collection"; "evidence" not "intel."
- **Decision criteria.** Outcomes clarity (what will I have at the end?); price predictability (proposal-template); confidentiality (NDA + DPA on day 1); team competence (founder bio + track record); time-to-value (engagement playbook; tier-aligned scope).
- **Evidence trusted.** Sales 8-slide deck; SOP-DISCOVERY_QUESTIONNAIRE_001 (HUMINT-grounded but presented as "structured research interview"); proposal-template; engagement-playbook; FOUNDER_BIO.md short-form.
- **First doubt.** "Are they going to disappear after the first invoice?" → addressed by engagement-playbook (cadence + checkpoints + outcomes-not-deliverables); MSA + SOW templates that signal serious legal posture; cycle-closure rhythm visible in `/governance` (operator-only — but signaled in pre-engagement materials).
- **I66 produces for them.** Sales 8-slide deck + `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md` (operator preps before each customer call). PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md (3-tier scope: entrepreneur / SME / enterprise). ENGAGEMENT_PLAYBOOK.md. SOP-DISCOVERY_QUESTIONNAIRE_001 (the structured interview the operator runs early). ## Cross-journey patterns

- **Operator (J-OP) is the only audience reading the internal-only register.** All other 6 audiences read the external register. The dual-register matrix exists to translate.
- **Investor (J-IN) + ENISA (J-ENISA) + Recruiter (J-RC) overlap on FOUNDER_BIO.md.** I66 ships three lengths × three languages = 9 versions of the bio. Per-audience FAQ reservoir handles the diverging follow-up questions.
- **Customer (J-CU) + Partner (J-PT) overlap on SERVICE_OFFERING_CATALOG.** Same source, different reading lens (customer reads "what can I buy?"; partner reads "where do I fit?"). The 6×3×3 matrix supports both.
- **Advisor (J-AD) is the lightest-touch audience.** Their decks are 4 slides; their materials are short. ADVOPS already has the canonicals; I66 doesn't duplicate, just cross-references.
- **All 7 audiences benefit from `.objections.md` + `.counterparty-brief.md` companions.** These are operator-private, drawn from baseline-reality matrix; not external-facing.
