## 1. Context

P3 of I70 ratified `D-IH-70-Q`: the legacy "Talent" role on `baseline_organisation.csv` is a monolith that owns Compliance methodology, HR operations, Ethics posture, and Learning curriculum simultaneously. The remediation: hard-remove the monolith and split into four single-responsibility sub-roles, three of them new (`Ethics Advisor`, `Learning Curator`, `People Operations Manager`) plus the pre-existing `Compliance` role re-scoped to "methodology IS the product" per its role-description.

P8.3 is the execution of D-IH-70-Q. This doc captures the migration audit trail (forward + rollback), mirroring the discipline established by `v3.1-schema-migration-log-2026-05-13.md` for the Marketing M3 redesign earlier the same day.

## 3. Rollback plan

To undo the People restructure (rolling baseline_organisation.csv + process_list.csv back to pre-P8.3 state):

1. `git revert <commit-sha-p83>` — single-commit revert (atomic by I70 commit discipline).
2. Run `validate_hlk.py` to confirm reverted state validates.
3. Run `scripts/sync_compliance_mirrors_from_csv.py` to push reverted CSVs to Supabase mirrors.
4. Update `tests/test_hlk.py::test_role_with_special_chars` back to `Ethics & Learning` reference.
5. Update `GOVERNANCE_MOAT.md` + `deck_slides.yaml` metrics back to 1.135 / 74.

The DDL columns (`sub_area`, `status`) added at P8.2 stay intact — they are nullable and don't block legacy schema reads.
