## 1. Thesis

Operations is Holistika's **delivery-capacity area** (`delivery_capacity` / Think Big /
COO). Other areas **govern** their domains; Operations **executes** the delivery spine
so a solo operator + AIC pair can run programs without improvising mechanics. The
area-completeness scorer at 70% with crit@L3 9/10 is accurate: registry-rich,
execution-poor. Closing AREA-03 (delivery doctrine) + AREA-02 (root charter) is the
tier unblock; AREA-09 pairing cliff is forward debt tranched at P2.
