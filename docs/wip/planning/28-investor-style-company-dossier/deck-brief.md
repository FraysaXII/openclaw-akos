# Deck brief — Holistika Research Company Dossier

**Document owner**: Brand Manager + Founder
**Version**: 0.1 (P0 of Initiative 28)
**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Status**: Working draft — feeds P1 narrative SSOT
**Audience**: ENISA adviser, ENISA / startup-certification reader, investor-style reader

---

## 1. Single sentence promise

Holística Research turns messy company operations into systems that can be executed, automated, audited, and improved — and we ship the software that proves it.

## 2. The deck must answer (in this order, in 5 minutes of reading)

1. What is the company actually doing today?
2. Why does that matter to anyone?
3. How do we know they can deliver?
4. What does it look like when it scales?
5. Why is this the right moment?
6. Who buys this, and how do they pay?
7. What would the reader confirm or do next?

## 3. Audience reads (what each one is testing)

| Reader | What they want to confirm | Failure mode if we miss it |
|:---|:---|:---|
| ENISA adviser (e.g. Guillermo) | Holistika has a real R&D + technology + scalability story; the *objeto social* / CNAE narrative will hold up; the founder is organised | They flag the file as "another consultancy applying for ENISA" and the certification stalls |
| ENISA / startup-certification reader | Empresa Emergente criteria are met (innovation, scalability, Spain nexus, operating viability); the case is verifiable | Certification is denied or downgraded |
| Investor-style reader | Real product, real customers/partners, capital efficiency, a credible path from service to product | They lose interest before slide 6 |

The deck must satisfy all three with one artefact, not three. Where audiences pull in different directions, the deck **leads with the investor frame** (because it forces sharper claims) and **lands ENISA fit explicitly in slide 13**.

## 4. Promise (one paragraph version, in Spanish)

> Holística Research diseña sistemas operativos para empresas que necesitan ordenar su operación antes de escalar. Combinamos investigación aplicada, ingeniería empresarial e ingeniería de procesos, y los productizamos en software propio (KiRBe). En 2025-2026 hemos puesto en producción cinco entregas distintas — sitio + CRM, ERP interno, plataforma SaaS de gestión de conocimiento, app B2B para partner y scaffold gobernado para otro partner — todas sobre la misma pila técnica y la misma disciplina de calidad.

## 7. The ask (slide 14)

Three asks in priority order, picked by audience:

1. **For ENISA adviser** — *"Confirmemos por escrito el paquete CNAE + objeto social y el reparto documental antes de la firma."*
2. **For certifier-style reader** — *"Revisar el dossier y emitir comentarios sobre encaje con criterios de Empresa Emergente."*
3. **For investor-style reader** — *"Conversación de 30 minutos para profundizar en el plan de productización 6-12 meses."*

The deck closes with the first by default; the others are alternative slide-14 variants kept for future audiences.

## 8. What the deck refuses to do (anti-patterns)

- No internal codenames, register names, plane labels, or row IDs.
- No `TODO[OPERATOR]` callouts in slide copy.
- No paragraph blocks that explain how we work internally — readers do not buy process; they buy outcomes.
- No partner-name banners. Partner identities appear once, in the proof captions, low-key.
- No table of canonical files. The deck is not a documentation index.
- No PESTEL / SWOT decks-as-form-filling. Market context appears as one short slide, not as four-quadrant grids.
- No fundraising-only "Series Seed / 18 months runway" slide unless the audience is an investor.

## 9. Tone reference (one Spanish paragraph)

Voice: *peer experto que retira la incertidumbre sin actuar la experiencia*. Frases cortas, claims verificables, números concretos, sin adverbios decorativos. Cuando hay opciones, hay recomendación. Cuando hay riesgo, hay mitigación. Nada de "estamos encantados de" ni "transformamos" ni "sinergias".

## 10. Visual brief

- Cover: dark hero band with subtle teal/amber gradient, oversized serif-feel-on-sans title, monogram top-left.
- Section openers (Problema, Insight, Solución, Proof, Mercado, Modelo, Roadmap, ENISA, Ask): big two-digit number in faded teal, single sentence in white-on-charcoal, full-bleed.
- Content slides: cream background, single column (not two), one big takeaway above the fold, supporting evidence below.
- Capability slides: 5 cards on one slide, each card 2 lines + 3 tags + footer (no long paragraphs in cards on the deck — long form lives in the appendix).
- Roadmap: horizontal three-band timeline (0–6, 6–12, 12–24 months), each band with a single phrase + 2 deliverables.
- Stat callouts: oversized number in teal, tiny uppercase label below, max 4 per slide.
- Footer (every body slide): `Holística Research · Dossier · 2026 · página X / Y` in small-caps, charcoal-muted.
