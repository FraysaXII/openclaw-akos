## Purpose

Execute the 5-commit research-first wave ratified at the operator's 2026-05-27 `Option A + C2 goes first` ratify gate. The wave rejects the prior session's framing (single-purpose investor stability dossier as a wave-deliverable) in favour of the broader strategic-posture work the operator named: **research-grounded reshape of brand-ops governance + marketing lifecycle disambiguation + investor-persona segmentation + mktops + channel doctrine activations + investor brief outputs as a downstream output of the research-grounded framework, not as a standalone artifact.**

Per the operator's verbatim framing from the 2026-05-27 message (the load-bearing doctrine surfacing):

> *"i'm talking about using what research we can (and extend what we must do) to actually help us do a live research for our strategic assets of our company. we are people who will go saying our NAME is R&D, how can i not come as donde-r and ask for a deep research to enrich, prove, disprove, discover, ingest, reference, optimize, wire, whatever we want to do with our plans?"*

> *"research goes always first and give to the organisation what we need strategically tactically and operationally, then the areas do their thing"*

And:

> *"i really can't say 6 because i asked for a research for those things too. why would we have in those research if i can't use it to take decisions. what can i flex about when i flex we're a research based company"*

The verbatim quotes name two load-bearing claims that govern this entire tranche:

1. **Research-first as identity-anchor.** Holistika's NAME is R&D. Strategic / tactical / operational decisions are research-grounded, not opinion-grounded or precedent-grounded alone. This pipeline IS the operationalisation of the existing `akos-applied-research-discipline.mdc` RULE 1-3 + `RESEARCH_HEAD_DISCIPLINE.md` doctrine — but it has not been promoted from craft-on-individual-agent to org-wide strategic posture.
