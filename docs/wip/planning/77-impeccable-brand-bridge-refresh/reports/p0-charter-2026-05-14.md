## What `/impeccable teach` represents — and what we are

Solo founders run `/impeccable teach`, answer 5 questions in their own words (audience / voice-in-three-words / interface-feel / visual-references / anti-references), and get a `.impeccable.md` file with their personal opinions. One founder, one app, one 5-minute interview.

Holistika is the institutional inverse: 15+ canonical SSOTs governing voice + visual + audience across a Branded House topology (Holistika umbrella + Holistika R&S + Think Big + HLK Tech Lab sub-marks + product brands MADEIRA / KiRBe / ENVOY); 16 personas, 10 channels, 4 persistence channels, 6 acquisition channels, 11+ artifact classes; a multilingual contract (FR/ES/EN); a voice-drift-triage SOP (`tbi_mkt_prc_voice_drift_triage_001`); a coming Pack A1 strict-day-1 validator. The bridge ARCHITECTURE Impeccable recommends (thin redirect to project SSOT) is exactly correct for our scale; the bridge CONTENT needs refresh + completion to reflect the institutional corpus.

This is also a **forward-charter seed for I74 (Brand Tooling Productization)**: Holistika canonicals × Impeccable discovery shape = an "AI-skill brand-DNA injector" — exactly what `@holistika/akos-brand` Strand A would package when TRIGGER-2 fires (≥2 external orgs request the methodology library). I77 prototypes the injection pattern internally first.
