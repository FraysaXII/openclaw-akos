### D-IH-75-G — Research area logic change (CORPINT lifecycle as the organizing spine)
**Question.** The previous structure carried two unreconciled organizing ideas — the four
disciplines (crafts) and the CORPINT lifecycle (flow) — leaving three lifecycle stages (share,
recall, protect) homeless. Reorganize?
**Decision.** Yes (founder-ratified 2026-05-29). Elevate the CORPINT information lifecycle
(acquire → process → store ⇄ recall → share, guarded by protect) to the area's **organizing spine**;
reframe the four disciplines as the **crafts** that move information through it; make cross-area
**joins** first-class (Store→Tech, Share→Marketing, Recall→Tech, Protect→People/Ethics+Compliance).
Mint [`RESEARCH_LIFECYCLE_DOCTRINE.md`](../../../references/hlk/v3.0/Research/canonicals/RESEARCH_LIFECYCLE_DOCTRINE.md);
delete the 6 empty technique husks + rebuild the discipline sub-areas as capability-seeded indexes.
Grounded externally (intelligence cycle + KM lifecycle + SCIP/counter-intelligence) per
`akos-applied-research-discipline.mdc` RULE 2.
**Owner.** Founder. **Reversibility.** Medium (markdown + folders + CSV; git-reversible).
**Status.** Active. **Reframes (does not discard)** A/C/D/E — the per-discipline SOP buildout now
hangs off named lifecycle stages.

### D-IH-75-H — PROTECT as a first-class lifecycle stage + counter-intelligence cross-area triad
**Question.** PROTECT (guarding sources + crown-jewel knowledge) was a "thin gap." Give it a home?
**Decision.** Yes (founder-ratified 2026-05-29). Establish PROTECT as a first-class CORPINT
lifecycle stage with a named cross-area ownership triad: Research/Intelligence (source protection +
GOI/POI stance) + People/Ethics (the ethical red lines) + People/Compliance (access levels +
confidence + redaction). **Forward-charter** a full Counter-Intelligence discipline mint,
co-ratified by the People/Ethics + People/Compliance co-owners (tracked as `OPS-86-27`).
**Owner.** Founder + People co-owners. **Reversibility.** Medium. **Status.** Active (forward-charter).
