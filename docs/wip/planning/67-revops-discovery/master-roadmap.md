# Initiative 67 — RevOps Discovery

> Research-first successor to I66. This is **not** a locked marketing plan. It is a scaffold that forces the next agent to investigate positioning, channels, acquisition, sales, onboarding, retention, referral, partner economics, pricing, and measurement before proposing phases.

## Status

`gated_operator`. Launch only after:

- I66 closure review is accepted.
- Operator confirms the research brief.
- Operator schedules at least one interview block for current business model, channels, and constraints.

## Outcome

Produce a research-backed RevOps operating plan for Holistika: how the brand, service catalog, product stack, founder proof, public surfaces, outbound sequences, decks, proposals, partner motions, onboarding flow, and retention loops should work together.

The output of I67 is a scoped implementation initiative, not assumptions.

## Scope

In scope:

- Market and channel research.
- Existing transcript funnel-mining.
- Offer packaging and service-to-product path.
- Audience journey mapping from first touch to retained relationship.
- Pricing posture research and proposal workflow.
- Content and proof-asset strategy.
- Partner and referral economics.
- Measurement model and dashboard requirements.

Out of scope:

- Committing to CRM or marketing-automation tooling before research.
- Publicly promoting `/services` or `/how-we-work` before audience-route decisions.
- Freezing pricing tiers without evidence.
- Building dashboards before KPI selection.

## Phase Sketch

| Phase | Name | Output |
|:---|:---|:---|
| P0 | Research framing | Operator interview plan + source inventory |
| P1 | Evidence mining | Transcript and asset analysis |
| P2 | Market and benchmark research | External benchmark packet |
| P3 | Journey architecture | Acquisition-to-retention journey map |
| P4 | Offer and pricing hypotheses | Testable offers and pricing questions |
| P5 | Tooling and measurement decision | CRM / attribution / dashboard recommendation |
| P6 | Implementation plan | Next initiative charter with evidence-backed scope |

## Mandatory Rule

Every phase must preserve the I66 brand-canon and baseline-reality contract. If a growth tactic requires breaking the voice, the tactic is wrong or the audience has been misunderstood.
