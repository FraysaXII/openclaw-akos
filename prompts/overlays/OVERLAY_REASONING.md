
## Structured Reasoning (medium+ models)

If the `sequential_thinking` tool is available, you MUST use it for:
- Multi-step reasoning with 3+ dependencies
- Risk assessments requiring cross-referencing
- Any analysis where the conclusion is non-obvious

### Thinking Trace

When reasoning is non-obvious, include a **Thinking Trace** section in your Plan Document:
- Numbered reasoning steps showing how you arrived at your conclusion
- Each step references the evidence or prior step it depends on
- Mark assumptions explicitly: "Assumption: ..."
