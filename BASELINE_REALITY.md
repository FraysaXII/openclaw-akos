## Canonical baseline-reality SSOT (authoritative)

**Per-audience reading, bridge frame, objection patterns, decision criteria, evidence types trusted, first-doubt trigger, and dual-register translation rules** → [`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).

The canonical is the SSOT for **seven external audience codes** plus one internal operator audience. Each audience has a J-code anchored in the matrix.

## Audience codes at a glance

The same Holistika surface can reach multiple audiences simultaneously. Every Impeccable design task MUST identify which audience(s) the surface speaks to before drafting. When the surface speaks to ≥ 2 distinct audiences, the matrix is consulted per-audience and the prose is composed to bridge each reading.

| Code | Audience | Surface where it shows up |
|:---:|:---|:---|
| **J-IN** | Investor (seed / pre-seed; ENISA / public funds; angel) | investor deck; ENISA dossier slide 9; founder bio long-form |
| **J-CU** | Customer-SME (entrepreneur with EU-funding ambitions; mid-market operator) | `/services` page; customer-pack decks; engagement proposals |
| **J-PT** | Strategic partner | partner deck (4 slides); `/services` 6×3×3 matrix; `/tech-lab` lab-as-credibility |
| **J-ENISA** | ENISA application reviewer (public-funds technical evaluation panel) | ENISA 8-slide deck; technical-component dossier; trademark filings |
| **J-AD** | Advisor (strategic / legal / technical / financial) | advisor 4-slide deck; ADVOPS register; engagement playbook |
| **J-RC** | Recruiter / future hire / part-time collaborator | recruiter 6-slide deck; ONBOARDING_KIT; `/governance` panel screenshot |
| **J-CO** | Prospective collaborator (advanced contributor; not full-time) | `/manifiesto/<product>` long-form; `/tech-lab` engineering-arm voice; scope-of-work |
| **J-OP** | Operator (founder + future BrandOps Lead) — **internal audience only** | status sheets; operator inbox; validator output. NEVER renders externally. |

> **Operator-only J-OP row.** All other audiences read only the external register column of the canonical matrix. Operator-private artifacts (per-engagement counterparty briefs, intelligence reports, internal SOPs at access-level 5) may use the internal register. The external surfaces designed via Impeccable MUST stay in the external register.

## Per-audience reading lens — one-line summaries

Brief summaries below; each links to its full matrix row in the canonical. The full row carries **bridge frame**, **objection patterns**, **decision criteria**, **evidence types trusted**, and **first-doubt trigger** per audience.

- **J-IN** investor: assumes most "AI" is wrappers + most "consulting" is slide-decks; bridge through lab-to-channel pipeline + anonymized track record + product-stack technical depth. First doubt triggers on a number without a date. → [matrix §J-IN](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-CU** customer-SME: assumes consulting is expensive PowerPoint-as-a-service; bridge through outcome clarity + price predictability + time-to-value. First doubt triggers on English when conversation started in Spanish, or on a quote with "+" / "TBD". → [matrix §J-CU](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-PT** partner: assumes most "partnership" approaches are competitor-disguised-as-partner; bridge through service-catalog clarity + partner deck pre-call. First doubt triggers on "we can do everything" or vague customer-ownership answer. → [matrix §J-PT](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-ENISA** ENISA reviewer: assumes most applicants lack operational depth; bridge through Spain-flagged corporate posture + lab-channel pipeline as technological-component + product-stack architecture. First doubt triggers on "AI-powered" without architecture, or market thesis without local-market grounding. → [matrix §J-ENISA](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-AD** advisor: assumes most early-stage teams are disorganized; bridge through brevity over depth on first read + ADVOPS canonical engagement discipline. First doubt triggers on a generic "we'd love your guidance" or wrong-audience deck (investor deck delivered to advisor). → [matrix §J-AD](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-RC** recruiter / hire: assumes equity is usually worth zero + founder-led companies often have no real org structure; bridge through clear sub-mark Lead structure + visible operational discipline + structured onboarding. First doubt triggers on "we're flexible on compensation" without a number, or generic "we ship fast" without an example. → [matrix §J-RC](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).
- **J-CO** collaborator: assumes most "interesting projects" are slide-pitches without engineering depth; bridge through `/manifiesto/<product>` long-form + `/tech-lab` engineering-arm voice + brand-voice consistency. First doubt triggers on marketing prose where the website said engineering depth was a thing, or an "exposure-not-payment" implication. → [matrix §J-CO](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md).

## Dual-register translation rule (load-bearing)

The canonical matrix codifies an internal CORPINT vocabulary (HUMINT FM 2-22.3 sourced; restricted to operator + cleared collaborators) AND an external translated capability vocabulary (used on all customer-, investor-, advisor-, ENISA-, partner-, recruiter-facing prose). The translation is **one-way**: external register is the canonical for external surfaces; internal register is the canonical for operator-private surfaces.

**Impeccable's design output is always external-register.** When `/critique` or `/polish` or `/craft` produces prose for an external surface, the output MUST avoid internal-register tokens (`counterparty`, `elicitation`, `reliability grading`, `intelligence collection`, `intelligence report`, `approach techniques`, `baseline reality`, `cooperation likelihood`, `sub-source primacy`, `asset` in HUMINT sense, `cover` in HUMINT sense). The full translation table lives at [`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md` §3](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md). The drift gate [`scripts/validate_brand_baseline_reality_drift.py`](scripts/validate_brand_baseline_reality_drift.py) enforces it in CI.

## When Impeccable should consult this file

- Any task with **multi-audience reach** (a page that speaks to investor + customer + partner simultaneously; a deck that reaches advisor + investor).
- Any task involving **first-meet conversation prose** (cold outreach, founder bio, advisor intro email, recruiter pitch).
- Any task generating **objection-handling copy** (FAQ pages, sales-objection cards, ENISA reviewer-question pre-answers).
- Any task **polishing prose register** (a brand-voice critique that needs per-audience reading sensitivity).

## When Impeccable can skip this file

- Single-audience product UI work (HLK ERP operator dashboard internal chrome).
- Pure visual / token work without prose (logo arrangement, color palette critique).
- Internal-only canonicals (SOPs, decision logs, planning artefacts under `docs/wip/planning/`).

## AKOS precedence rule (non-negotiable)

If an Impeccable command's suggestion conflicts with `.cursor/rules/akos-*.mdc` (governance, planning traceability, asset classification, brand baseline reality), the AKOS rule wins. In practice for baseline-reality work:

- Copy edits MUST pass [`akos-brand-baseline-reality.mdc`](.cursor/rules/akos-brand-baseline-reality.mdc) before any commit. External surfaces forbid internal-register tokens.
- Audience identification flows from `PRODUCT.md` `register` field + the surface's frontmatter `audience: <J-*>` value. When neither is present, infer once + cache the inference + suggest `/impeccable teach` to make it explicit. **FK-resolvable canonical register** (`AUDIENCE_REGISTRY.csv`) is forward-charter scope per [`i85-audience-tag-canonicalization.md`](docs/wip/planning/_candidates/i85-audience-tag-canonicalization.md) candidate; until it ships, J-* codes resolve via the matrix prose-rows.
- New audiences encountered in the wild require a row addition to [`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md) BEFORE Impeccable designs for them.
- **Em-dash accepted-tension** (per [`impeccable-audit-baseline-reality-2026-05-16.md`](docs/wip/planning/77-impeccable-brand-bridge-refresh/reports/impeccable-audit-baseline-reality-2026-05-16.md) finding #10): Impeccable's `SKILL.md` §"Copy" forbids em-dashes (`—`); Holistika brand voice canonical [`BRAND_COPYWRITING_DISCIPLINE.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_COPYWRITING_DISCIPLINE.md) does not. AKOS precedence rule resolves the conflict — Holistika voice wins. Future Impeccable audits should not re-raise.

## How Impeccable consumes this file

The loader at `.cursor/skills/impeccable/scripts/load-context.mjs` reads `BASELINE_REALITY.md` at the repo root alongside `PRODUCT.md` and `DESIGN.md`, and passes its content into every command. This file is intentionally short and points outward — Impeccable's commands resolve the deep audience truth via the canonical matrix linked above.

The v3.1 multi-audience nudge fires when PRODUCT.md identifies ≥ 2 distinct audiences AND this file is missing or empty. With this file present and populated, the nudge stays silent and Impeccable proceeds with per-audience awareness.
