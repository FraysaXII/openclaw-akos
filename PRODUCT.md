## Audience and product context (one-pager — full detail in the canonical files above)

**Who is the product for.** Seven external audience codes governed at [`BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md) — J-IN investor, J-CU customer-SME, J-PT strategic partner, J-ENISA ENISA reviewer, J-AD advisor, J-RC recruiter / future hire, J-CO prospective collaborator. The sibling bridge [`BASELINE_REALITY.md`](BASELINE_REALITY.md) surfaces a one-page summary per audience for Impeccable's multi-audience setup gate (v3.1+). Specific audience playbook for the ENISA / advisor track lives in [`docs/wip/planning/28-investor-style-company-dossier/deck-brief.md`](docs/wip/planning/28-investor-style-company-dossier/deck-brief.md).

**What we are.** Holistika Research designs operating systems for companies that need to order their operation before they scale. The Branded House topology (per `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md`): Holistika Research SL is the legal entity; Holistika is the umbrella brand; three sub-marks operate under it — Holistika R&S (research-and-strategy services), Think Big (founder-coaching arm), HLK Tech Lab (engineering arm). Product brands — MADEIRA (the AI O5-1 agent), KiRBe (knowledge surfacing system), ENVOY (channel adapter), plus two coming — sit under the Tech Lab sub-mark. Internal-first validation: we run our own products before we sell them. Five concrete production deliveries acknowledged in [Apéndice C of the appendix dossier](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/_assets/advops/2026-holistika-incorporation/enisa_evidence/dossier_es.md).

**Brand voice in three words** (from `BRAND_VOICE_FOUNDATION.md`): rigorous, structured, plain. The voice charter: *"Holistika speaks as the rigorous peer who removes uncertainty without performing expertise."* Plain words externally; jargon stays internal — that's the operational rule per `BRAND_JARGON_AUDIT.md`. Per-sub-mark voice tier (Tier 1 umbrella / Tier 2 sub-mark / Tier 3 product) per `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md` §"Voice tier mapping".

**What the interface should feel like.** Calm, trustworthy, fast. Operator confidence over flash. Cream-warm light surfaces; deep slate dark hero bands; teal `hsl(168 55% 38%)` and amber `hsl(38 80% 50%)` accents (never both as full backgrounds — they are signal devices). Inter typography. Generous whitespace. Numbered section indicators (`01` / `02`) instead of decorative ornaments. Visual SSOT in sibling [`DESIGN.md`](DESIGN.md) bridge + canonical [`BRAND_VISUAL_PATTERNS.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_VISUAL_PATTERNS.md) + [`BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md).

**Visual references** (governed): the production marketing site sourced verbatim into [`BRAND_VISUAL_PATTERNS.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_VISUAL_PATTERNS.md). Logo and wordmark assets governed by [`BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Admin/O5-1/Marketing/Brand/canonicals/BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md). The Figma file inventory lives at [`FIGMA_FILES_REGISTRY.md`](docs/references/hlk/v3.0/Envoy%20Tech%20Lab/Repositories/FIGMA_FILES_REGISTRY.md).

**Anti-references** (what we explicitly are not):

- Performative consultancy: *"We are delighted to announce..."* — refused (`BRAND_DO_DONT.md`).
- Stack-jargon dump: *"FastAPI + pgvector + Cohere reranking + Logfire..."* externally — replaced with outcome statements per `BRAND_JARGON_AUDIT.md` §5.
- LLM-corporate tone tells: triadic abstract-noun stacks, false singularity, contrastive prose ("not X but Y"), repeated openings — strict-day-1 catalogued per `BRAND_LLM_TONE_TELLS.md` (I71 P1).
- Internal CORPINT register externally: `counterparty`, `elicitation`, `reliability grading`, `intelligence report`, `cooperation likelihood`, `asset` in HUMINT sense, `cover` in HUMINT sense — restricted to operator-private surfaces per `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md` §3 dual-register rule.
- Stock-photography hero shots, clip-art icons, gradient backdrops as decoration, more than two accent colors per slide.
- Internal codenames externally (`AKOS`, `topic_*`, `plane`, `ADVOPS`, `GOI/POI`, `ref_id`, etc.) — full forbidden list in `BRAND_JARGON_AUDIT.md` §4.
- Ad-hoc abbreviations for sub-marks (`HRS`, `HRL`, etc.) — only canonical short forms per `BRAND_ABBREVIATIONS.md`.

## Register

This file is the **brand**-register surface for Impeccable: external-facing dossiers, decks, marketing pages, partner-facing material, public manifestos. The **product**-register surfaces (HLK ERP operator dashboard, KiRBe SaaS UI, MADEIRA agent UI) live in their respective product repos and have their own DESIGN-equivalent files. Per-surface register identification flows per `BRAND_REGISTER_MATRIX.md` (relationship × channel → tonal register).

When a surface speaks to ≥ 2 audiences simultaneously (a `/services` page reaching customer + partner + investor; a manifesto reaching collaborator + recruiter), the [`BASELINE_REALITY.md`](BASELINE_REALITY.md) bridge + canonical matrix MUST be consulted per-audience.

## AKOS precedence rule (non-negotiable)

If an Impeccable command's suggestion conflicts with `.cursor/rules/akos-*.mdc` (governance, planning traceability, asset classification, jargon audit, brand foundation, baseline reality, executable process catalog), the AKOS rule wins. In practice:

- Copy edits suggested by Impeccable MUST pass `BRAND_JARGON_AUDIT.md` §4 + `BRAND_BASELINE_REALITY_MATRIX.md` §3 dual-register translation rules + `BRAND_LLM_TONE_TELLS.md` lexical signatures before any commit.
- Visual / token suggestions MUST use names from `BRAND_VISUAL_PATTERNS.md` §1 + logo assets from `BRAND_LOGO_SYSTEM.md`; raw hex codes are forbidden when a token exists.
- Layout / motion suggestions stay phase-scoped per `akos-planning-traceability.mdc`; one commit per phase.
- Sub-mark voice tier suggestions MUST match `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md` §"Voice tier mapping" for the relevant sub-mark; mixing Tier-1 umbrella voice into Tier-3 product surfaces (or vice versa) is a brand-architecture violation.

## How Impeccable consumes this file

The loader at `.cursor/skills/impeccable/scripts/load-context.mjs` reads `PRODUCT.md` + `DESIGN.md` + `BASELINE_REALITY.md` at the repo root and passes their content into every command. This file is intentionally short and points outward — Impeccable's commands resolve the deep brand truth via the canonicals linked above.

The v3.1 multi-audience setup gate (per `.cursor/skills/impeccable/SKILL.md` line 8) checks `BASELINE_REALITY.md` presence when PRODUCT.md identifies ≥ 2 distinct audiences. With the sibling bridge present and populated, the gate stays silent and Impeccable proceeds with per-audience awareness.
